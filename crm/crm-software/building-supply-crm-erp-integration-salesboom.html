The Challenge: Disconnected Systems and Complex Operations

Building supply and distribution companies face operational complexity that traditional CRM or ERP systems alone cannot solve. Each project involves multiple stakeholders—contractors, architects, homeowners—and demands real-time coordination across sales, inventory, delivery, and service.

Common pain points include:

  • Fragmented data between sales, accounting, and warehouse systems
  • Difficulty tracking quotes, bids, and negotiated pricing
  • Limited visibility into inventory across multiple locations
  • Manual order entry leading to errors and delays
  • Inconsistent customer communication and service follow-up
  • Lack of consolidated reporting and forecasting tools

These silos lead to inefficiency, customer frustration, and lost revenue. The solution lies in complete CRM and ERP integration designed for the realities of the building supply industry.

A Unified CRM + ERP Solution Purpose-Built for Building Supply

Salesboom delivers a fully integrated CRM and ERP platform engineered to meet the unique challenges of building supply companies. By connecting sales, service, inventory, and logistics into one intelligent system, Salesboom enables teams to operate seamlessly from quote to delivery.

Key integration capabilities:

  • Centralized customer and contractor database with full history
  • Real-time synchronization between sales orders, inventory, and accounting
  • Streamlined quote-to-order workflows with automated pricing updates
  • Connected financial management for invoices, receivables, and payments
  • Project tracking tools linking bids, materials, and deliveries
  • Unified reporting and KPIs across departments

Key Insight

This all-in-one solution gives management a 360° view of operations, improving decision-making and efficiency across the supply chain.
