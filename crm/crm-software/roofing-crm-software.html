Why Traditional Roofing Operations Hold Your Business Back

Today's roofing companies struggle with disconnected systems that create operational chaos. Paper processes, manual spreadsheets, and juggling multiple tools waste valuable hours that could be spent on actual roofing work.

The Cost of Inefficiency

Your crews spend more time managing customer information, creating estimates, tracking jobs, and handling billing than they do generating revenue:

  • Hours wasted manually updating spreadsheets for job scheduling
  • Crews arriving at wrong addresses or missing critical job details
  • Lost revenue from quotes that never get followed up
  • Customer frustration from poor communication and missed appointments
  • Duplicate data entry between your CRM, scheduling tools, and QuickBooks
  • No real-time visibility into crew locations or job status
  • Follow-up calls falling through the cracks as jobs age without resolution

What Modern Roofing Businesses Need

Success requires integrated solutions that connect all aspects of your operation:

  • Customer management integrated with job scheduling
  • GPS tracking and visual map-based job planning
  • Mobile quoting and invoicing from the field
  • Automated QuickBooks synchronization
  • Real-time crew coordination and dispatch
  • Elimination of manual work and duplicate data entry
  • Everyone synchronized with the same accurate information

Visual Job Scheduling with GPS Map Integration

See all your roofing jobs plotted on Google Maps with complete job information embedded in each map pin. Schedule efficiently by geography, eliminate unnecessary drive time, and give crews instant access to daily routes on their mobile devices.

Intelligent Job Plotting

Every job automatically appears as a pin on Google Maps with embedded data including customer name, address, contact information, job notes, invoice number, route number, map grid location, and job status. Click any pin to see complete job details and navigate directly to the location.

Map Grid Organization

Organize jobs by custom map grids that correspond to your service territories. Group jobs geographically to minimize drive time, optimize fuel costs, and maximize the number of jobs completed per day. Prioritize jobs by grid location, age, and status.

Real-Time Schedule Updates

As job information updates in the system, changes sync in near real-time to your maps and reports. Crews always see current information—no outdated printouts or confusion about which address to go to next.

Crew Schedule Sharing

Crews log in online to view jobs plotted on Google Maps and access their daily and weekly work schedules. Each crew sees their assigned jobs organized by truck number, so everyone knows exactly what truck they're taking and which jobs they're completing that day.

Smart Job Tracking That Prevents Work From Aging

Track every roofing job from quote to completion with customizable data fields and intelligent prioritization that ensures urgent work gets done first while preventing jobs from aging indefinitely.

Comprehensive Job Data Capture

Capture detailed information for each roofing job with standard and custom fields:

  • Date Dispatched and Date Approved
  • Customer email and contact information
  • Route number and map grid assignment
  • ZIP code for territory management
  • Invoice number with auto-generation
  • Truck number and crew assignment
  • Job priority and aging status
  • Project category and subcategory
  • Custom fields for your specific workflows

Mobile Quoting and Invoicing for Field Crews

Generate professional quotes and invoices on-site from smartphones or tablets. Close more deals by providing immediate pricing while still at the customer's location, eliminating delays and improving conversion rates.

On-Site Quote Generation

Sales reps and crew leaders create professional branded quotes from the job site using mobile devices. Include itemized pricing, photos, material specifications, and payment terms—all while the customer watches.

Instant Invoice Creation

Generate invoices immediately upon job completion from mobile devices. Collect payment details, capture signatures electronically, and sync automatically to QuickBooks—eliminating billing delays and improving cash flow.

Custom Branded Templates

Design professional quote and invoice templates that reflect your brand identity. Include your logo, colors, terms and conditions, and custom messaging. Multiple templates available for different service types or customer segments.

Seamless QuickBooks Integration

Eliminate duplicate data entry between your CRM and accounting software. Real-time bidirectional sync ensures customer, job, and financial data stays perfectly synchronized between Salesboom and QuickBooks.

Bidirectional Data Sync

Customer information, jobs, invoices, payments, and items sync automatically between the CRM and QuickBooks. Changes made in either system update the other in real-time, eliminating manual reconciliation and reducing errors.

  • Customer and vendor records sync automatically
  • Invoices created in CRM appear in QuickBooks instantly
  • Payment status updates flow both directions
  • Job costing data integrates with financial reporting

Native Integration Architecture

Our QuickBooks integration uses official Intuit APIs for reliable, secure connectivity. Unlike third-party middleware solutions that require ongoing maintenance, our native integration delivers consistent performance without breaking.

  • Works with QuickBooks Desktop and Online
  • Secure OAuth authentication
  • Automatic error detection and resolution
  • No additional middleware costs
