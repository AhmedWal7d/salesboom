A Seamless Sales-to-Operations Workflow

Salesboom Construction CRM connects business development with delivery by maintaining linked records across the entire lifecycle. Information captured during early client engagement is not lost during project handover or execution.

Lead Job Bid / Estimate Execution Ready-to-Invoice

Lead Stage

Initial contact information, project requirements, timeline expectations, budget parameters, and qualification notes are captured in the CRM. Sales teams can track multiple opportunities with the same customer across different project types.

Job Stage

Qualified leads convert to jobs with all relevant context maintained. Customer preferences, site conditions, special requirements, and historical notes carry forward automatically. Jobs become the central operational record.

Bid / Estimate Stage

Bids and estimates are created directly from jobs, maintaining the connection to the original customer and project requirements. Multiple estimates can be tracked with version control, approval workflows, and acceptance status.

Execution Stage

Accepted estimates convert into active jobs automatically, carrying forward all relevant data. Project teams have instant access to customer commitments, specifications, and history. Tasks, appointments, and notes ensure accountability and continuity.

Ready-to-Invoice Stage

Completed work is marked ready for invoicing with complete documentation and traceability. Management has real-time visibility into work that can be billed, supporting proactive cash-flow management.

This connected workflow eliminates the information loss and communication gaps that plague construction companies using disconnected systems or manual handoffs.
