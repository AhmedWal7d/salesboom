The Three-Layer Autonomous Architecture

A revolutionary operating system for enterprise AI

The Salesboom Stack is built on a revolutionary three-layer architecture that transforms how AI integrates with business operations. This isn't just software—it's an autonomous operating system.

1 Layer 1: The Intelligence Layer (The "Brain")

At the top sits the intelligence layer that orchestrates all actions across your business:

Promptuit (Prompt Engine)

A specialized layer for engineering and managing the prompts that feed into the AI. Promptuit acts as the "legislative branch" of your AI operations, enforcing governance, compliance, and quality control on every AI interaction.

Salesboom Chatbot Assistants

Conversational agents that serve as the primary interface for both internal users and external customers. These aren't simple chatbots—they're intelligent assistants that understand context, access complete business data, and execute complex workflows.

Agent Management System (AMS)

This is the game-changing differentiator. The AMS is the orchestration layer responsible for spawning, monitoring, and managing Autonomous Agents that execute tasks without continuous human oversight. These agents can reach down into the CRM/ERP to perform tasks like creating quotes, logging support tickets, and updating accounting records—all autonomously.

2 Layer 2: The Operations Layer (The Core)

The middle section represents daily business workflow, all centralized in the Hub (CRM + ERP):

The Demandinator

The demand generation system where a human sets the strategy, and the AI agents take over execution. Agents actively source leads, initiate marketing workflows, and qualify prospects automatically.

Unified Revenue Lifecycle Management

The central nervous system connects all standard business departments in a continuous feedback loop:

  • Marketing & Sales work together seamlessly with shared data and automated handoffs
  • Support teams have complete visibility into customer history and can trigger sales opportunities
  • Orders and Quotes flow automatically from qualified conversations
  • Accounting receives real-time updates as deals progress, eliminating data entry

Circular Intelligence Flow

Notice that data doesn't flow in one direction. Marketing data informs sales strategies, sales interactions inform support priorities, and support conversations create upsell opportunities—all automatically through the Agent Management System.

3 Layer 3: The Infrastructure Layer (Data & Integrations)

The foundation powers the system through two critical components:

Data Engine (AI + Data)

Creates a unified layer where data meets intelligence. Powered by models like Gemini, it processes unstructured data, enriches lead information, and connects to automation tools like Zapier. The Data Engine ensures every AI decision is based on complete, accurate, contextualized information.

Integration Station (AI + APIs)

The gateway to the outside world, connecting your internal ERP/CRM to external platforms seamlessly:

  • ChatGPT for advanced language processing and generation
  • Shopify for e-commerce transactions and inventory management
  • Outlook for email communication and calendar coordination
  • n8n for sophisticated workflow automation
  • Custom API connections for your existing business tools

Hybrid Intelligence Approach: This uses multiple AI models alongside logic-based automation, employing a "best-of-breed" strategy where different tools are used for different strengths.

Promptuit: The AI Governance and Lifecycle Management System

From prompts to revenue with complete governance

Promptuit is far more than a prompt library—it's a comprehensive "Prompt-to-Revenue" operating system that manages the entire lifecycle of Generative AI interactions. It combines software governance with human expertise to ensure AI is safe, effective, and profit-driven.

The Triple-Audit Architecture

To solve the "black box" problem of AI hallucinations, Promptuit maintains a synchronized audit trail across three systems simultaneously:

1. The CRM Audit

Logs the prompt against the specific Customer/Deal record, creating accountability

2. The Data Engine Audit

Logs the raw data inputs used to generate the prompt, ensuring traceability

3. The AMS Audit

Logs the agent's execution path, documenting every decision point

Result: Complete transparency—"rewind" any hallucination to see exactly which prompt version or data point caused the error and recover immediately.

Real-Time Contextual Builder

As users type, the Prompt Builder auto-populates variables using real-time data from the CRM (People, Products, Deals). But it doesn't just "fill in the blanks"—it applies Workflow Rules to ensure compliance:

  • If a user tries to send a quote, the Builder enforces the "Margin Management Rule" to ensure the prompt includes profit safeguards
  • The AI literally cannot send a non-compliant quote because the rule forbids the prompt structure
  • Dynamic context assembly from multiple data sources
  • Real-time validation against business rules
  • Automated compliance checking at prompt level

Governance First: AI safety is built into the architecture, not added as an afterthought.

Three Types of Autonomous Agents

Specialized AI workforce executing business processes

Listener Agents

Purpose: Monitor the world for relevant triggers

Examples:

  • Email Listener watches for inbound emails, parses them, and triggers workflows
  • Calendar Listener detects upcoming meetings and prepares briefing materials
  • Social Media Listener monitors brand mentions and customer sentiment
  • Database Listener watches for record changes and initiates actions
Decider Agents

Purpose: Evaluate data and make recommendations

Examples:

  • Lead Scoring Agent analyzes prospect data and assigns qualification scores
  • Pricing Optimization Agent recommends optimal pricing based on competitive intelligence
  • Risk Assessment Agent evaluates deals for potential issues
  • Forecasting Agent predicts pipeline outcomes using historical patterns
Executor Agents

Purpose: Take action in external systems

Examples:

  • Quote Generation Agent creates proposals and sends them automatically
  • Invoice Creator Agent generates and dispatches invoices when deals close
  • Email Responder Agent drafts and sends contextual customer responses
  • Calendar Booking Agent schedules meetings based on availability

The Power of Agent Orchestration

These agents work together as a coordinated workforce, with the AMS managing handoffs, monitoring execution, and ensuring human oversight where needed.
