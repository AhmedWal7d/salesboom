Introducing Email Manager: Your CRM Lives in Your Inbox

Email Manager transforms how sales teams handle customer communications by embedding CRM functionality directly into Gmail and Outlook.

When viewing any email in Outlook or Gmail, Email Manager instantly shows you whether the sender is currently a Contact in your CRM system. If they're not, add them with one click. If they are, attach the email to their contact record with one click. It's that simple.

Work Where You Already Work

Sales teams live in their email inbox. Email Manager meets you there, eliminating the need to constantly log into separate CRM systems just to save important information.

Key capabilities:

  • Instant contact recognition shows CRM status of every email sender
  • One-click contact creation adds new leads directly from email
  • One-click email attachment saves messages to contact records
  • Full functionality without requiring separate CRM login
  • Seamless experience in both Gmail and Outlook interfaces
  • Real-time sync ensures data is always current across systems

Two Tasks, Done Right

Email Manager recognizes that email triage and CRM management are two different tasks requiring different mindsets. The system separates these activities for maximum efficiency.

First, quickly mine your inbox for valuable contacts and important emails, saving them to the CRM instantly while you're in email mode. Then, schedule dedicated timean hour or twoto work in the CRM, categorizing contacts, planning follow-ups, and building relationship strategies.

This separation of tasks eliminates context-switching overhead and allows you to focus completely on one activity at a time, dramatically improving productivity and reducing mental fatigue.

Why Email Manager Outperforms Alternative Approaches

Many organizations struggle with email-to-CRM workflows, relying on inferior approaches that create more problems than they solve.

Better Than Manual Email Forwarding

Manual forwarding to CRM email addresses is slow, error-prone, and creates poor data quality. Email Manager automates the process with perfect accuracy and zero manual effort.

Better Than Browser Extensions

Third-party browser extensions are security risks, often break with email updates, and provide inconsistent experiences. Email Manager is native integration built on official APIs.

Better Than Separate CRM Login

Forcing users to log into CRM separately to save emails creates friction that kills adoption. Email Manager eliminates this friction by working entirely within the email interface.

Better Than CC'ing the CRM

CC'ing a CRM email address on every message is disruptive, trains customers to expect CRM in the loop, and captures irrelevant noise. Email Manager gives you selective control.

Better Than Export/Import Workflows

Exporting emails from email client and importing to CRM is complex, time-consuming, and rarely gets done. Email Manager makes it one click with no technical complexity.

Better Than Hoping Sales Remembers

Relying on sales reps to manually enter email information into CRM is wishful thinking. Email Manager makes the right behavior so easy it becomes automatic.
