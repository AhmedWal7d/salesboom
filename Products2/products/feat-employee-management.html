Empowering Employees Through Self-Service Technology

Salesboom's Employee Self-Service system transforms the employee experience by putting control directly in their hands. Using our intuitive web-based interface, employees can manage their own information, submit requests, and access resources—all without requiring HR intervention for routine tasks.

Update Personal Information Instantly

Employees can update their own records including address changes, bank information for direct deposit, emergency contacts, and personal details through a secure, user-friendly web portal. This eliminates the frustration of submitting paper forms and waiting for HR processing, while ensuring your employee database always contains current, accurate information.

Vacation and Time-Off Management

Request vacation days, paid time off, sick leave, and other absences directly through the portal with automated approval workflows. Employees can view their available balances, check team calendars to avoid conflicts, submit requests with a few clicks, and receive instant notifications when requests are approved—all without sending a single email.

Case Submission and Solution Review

Submit HR inquiries, IT support requests, facility issues, or any employee concern through the integrated case management system. Track case status in real-time, receive automated updates, and access a searchable knowledge base of solutions to common questions—empowering employees to find answers without waiting for HR responses.

Document Access and Management

Access employment documents, company policies, benefits information, tax forms, and training materials 24/7 through the secure employee portal. Download pay stubs, W-2 forms, and other essential documents without requesting copies from HR, ensuring employees always have the information they need when they need it.

Training and Development

View available training programs, enroll in courses, track learning progress, and access development resources all through the self-service portal. Employees take ownership of their professional development while HR gains visibility into training completion and skills development across the organization.

Performance and Goals

Access performance reviews, set personal goals, track achievement progress, and view career development plans. The transparent system ensures employees always understand expectations, can measure their progress, and have clarity on paths for advancement within the organization.
