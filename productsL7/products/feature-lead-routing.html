Flexible Routing Rules for Every Business Model

Build routing rules that match your business reality

Geographic Territory Routing

Automatically route leads based on country, state, province, city, postal code, or custom territory definitions. A prospect from Shanghai goes to your Asia-Pacific team, while a lead from Toronto routes to your Canadian representative. Territory boundaries are fully customizable and can overlap for shared coverage areas.

The system intelligently handles edge cases like remote territories, split coverage regions, and backup assignments when primary reps are unavailable. Update territory maps once in Salesboom, and all future leads route correctly without touching individual rules.

Role-Based Assignment

Route leads based on product interest, industry vertical, company size, or deal complexity. Enterprise prospects with 1,000+ employees automatically route to senior account executives, while small business leads go to inside sales representatives. Product-specific inquiries route to specialist teams with deep expertise.

Define as many role-based criteria as needed. A manufacturing company interested in your premium product tier triggers assignment to your industrial vertical team lead, ensuring expertise alignment from first contact.

Account Ownership Logic

Prevent territory conflicts by routing leads from existing customer accounts directly to the current account owner. When a new contact from Acme Corporation submits a form, the system checks for existing Acme records and assigns the lead to the rep already managing that relationship.

This protects existing customer relationships, eliminates internal disputes over lead ownership, and ensures continuity for prospects who may have previously interacted with your company through different contacts.

Round-Robin Distribution

Balance workload evenly across your team with intelligent round-robin rotation. Leads distribute sequentially through your sales team, ensuring fair opportunity allocation and preventing burnout from uneven assignment patterns.

Configure multiple round-robin pools for different lead sources, regions, or product lines. The system tracks assignment history and automatically skips unavailable reps, routing to the next person in rotation. Real-time dashboards show assignment counts to ensure perfect balance.

Beyond Basic Assignment: Complete Lead Lifecycle Automation

Routing is just the beginning of comprehensive automation

Instant Email Alerts

The moment a lead is assigned, the designated sales rep receives a real-time email notification with complete prospect details, lead source information, and captured form data. Alerts include direct links to the lead record for instant access.

Automatic Task Creation

Routing rules automatically generate follow-up tasks with due dates, priorities, and detailed instructions. Never lose a lead to follow-up failurethe system ensures every prospect receives timely attention.

SMS Notifications

For high-priority leads, trigger instant SMS alerts to sales reps. Critical opportunities get immediate attention with mobile notifications that break through email clutter.

Escalation Rules

If a lead isn't contacted within a specified timeframe, automatically escalate to a manager or reassign to another rep. Ensure no opportunity languishes unattended.

Lead Scoring Integration

Combine routing with lead scoring to prioritize high-value prospects. Hot leads with high scores trigger immediate assignment and priority alerts, while cold leads follow standard workflows.

Quality Control Checks

Validate lead data quality before routing. Check for required fields, detect duplicate records, and flag suspicious submissionsensuring only qualified leads reach your sales team.
