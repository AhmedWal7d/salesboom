Why Security Cannot Be an Afterthought in CRM Systems

The risks facing businesses today are severe:

  • Cyberattacks targeting customer databases increasing 300% year-over-year
  • Average data breach costs exceeding $4.5 million per incident
  • Regulatory compliance requirements (GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA) with significant penalties for violations
  • Customer expectation for enterprise-grade protection regardless of company size
  • Reputation damage that can take years to rebuild after security incidents
  • Loss of competitive advantage when confidential business data is exposed

With hosted CRM solutions, security becomes even more critical because your data lives outside your firewall. Fort Knox-Level Physical Security for Your Data

Digital security starts with physical security. Your data resides on production equipment collocated in Halifax, Nova Scotia, at a state-of-the-art facility specifically designed for maximum security and uptime.

24/7 Physical Access Control

The data center features multiple layers of physical security to prevent unauthorized access:

  • Biometric authentication and keycard access systems at all entry points
  • 24-hour on-site security personnel and video surveillance monitoring
  • Mantrap entry systems that prevent tailgating and unauthorized entry
  • Visitor logging and escort requirements for all non-authorized personnel
  • Secure, locked server cabinets with individual access controls
  • Environmental sensors detecting smoke, water, temperature, and humidity anomalies

These physical barriers ensure that even if digital defenses were somehow compromised, unauthorized individuals cannot physically access the servers containing your data.

Continuous Uptime Infrastructure

Beyond security, the facility is engineered for reliability and continuous operation:

  • Redundant electrical generators providing automatic failover during power outages
  • Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems bridging the gap during generator startup
  • Redundant data center air conditioning systems maintaining optimal temperature and humidity
  • Fire suppression systems using clean agent technology that protects equipment
  • Multiple upstream internet service providers ensuring connectivity redundancy
  • Seismic bracing and environmental controls protecting against natural disasters

This infrastructure ensures your CRM remains accessible 24/7/365, even during emergencies, natural disasters, or utility failures that would cripple standard office environments.

Military-Grade Encryption Protecting Data in Transit and at Rest

Salesboom leverages the strongest encryption products available to protect customer data and communications from interception, tampering, and unauthorized access.

SSL/TLS Encryption

128-bit SSL for All Data Transmission

Every interaction between your browser and Salesboom servers is protected by 128-bit Verisign SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) Certification using 1024-bit RSA public keys. This represents the same encryption standard used by financial institutions for online banking.

When you see the lock icon in your browser, you have visual confirmation that all data transmitted is fully encrypted and unreadable to interceptors.

Data at Rest Protection

Encrypted Storage Systems

Customer data stored on our servers is protected with encryption at rest, ensuring that even in the unlikely event of physical media theft, data remains unreadable without proper decryption keys.

Multiple encryption layers protect data throughout its lifecycle, from initial entry to long-term archival storage.

Certificate Management

Trusted Security Certificates

Salesboom uses security certificates from recognized certificate authorities, providing browser-verified authentication that confirms you're connecting to legitimate Salesboom servers, not imposter sites attempting to steal credentials.

Regular certificate renewal and monitoring ensure continuous protection without security gaps.
