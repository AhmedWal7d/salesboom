Why Traditional Quoting Processes Cost You Deals

Sales teams waste hours creating quotes in disconnected tools like Word, Excel, and email. This fragmented approach creates serious problems that directly impact revenue and customer satisfaction.

The Hidden Costs of Manual Quoting:

  • Sales reps spend 3-5 hours per week recreating quotes from scratch
  • Pricing errors and inconsistencies damage profit margins by 5-15%
  • Quotes get lost in email chains, requiring customers to ask "what happened to my quote?"
  • Manual data entry between quote and invoice systems introduces billing errors
  • No visibility into which quotes are pending, approved, or abandoned
  • Sales managers lack real-time forecasting based on quoted pipeline value
  • Finance teams struggle with revenue recognition and audit trails
  • Approved quotes sit in limbo waiting for someone to manually create invoices

The Solution

Every manual step in the quoting process creates friction, delays deals, and increases the risk of losing opportunities to faster competitors. Modern businesses need quote management software that eliminates manual work and creates a seamless Quote-to-Cash workflow.

The Salesboom Quote Management Advantage

Salesboom's Quote Management Software sits at the center of your Quote-to-Cash process, connecting opportunities, products, pricing, customers, and invoicing into one controlled, automated workflow.

Centralized Quote Creation and Management

The Quotes tab in Salesboom CRM provides a unified workspace for the entire sales quoting process. Sales reps access all quote-related information in one place, with complete context about the customer, opportunity, products, and pricing history.

Key capabilities include:

  • Auto-generated unique quote numbers for perfect tracking and audit trails
  • Direct linkage to opportunities and account records for complete deal context
  • Standardized product catalog with approved pricing to protect margins
  • Built-in tax calculation supporting HST, VAT, and multiple tax jurisdictions
  • Flexible discount controls with approval workflows for special pricing
  • Product cost tracking to monitor profitability on every quote
  • Shipping, handling, and other cost management for accurate totals
  • Real-time quote status tracking from creation through approval to conversion

Professional Quote Delivery

First impressions matter. Salesboom generates beautiful, branded PDF quotes that reinforce your professionalism and make it easy for customers to say yes.

Quote delivery features:

  • One-click PDF generation using your branded templates
  • Direct email delivery from within the CRM without switching tools
  • Mail merge functionality for personalized, high-volume quoting
  • Custom quote validity windows with automatic expiration dates
  • Professional formatting that adapts to your brand guidelines
  • Mobile-responsive quote viewing for customers on any device

Seamless Quote-to-Invoice Conversion
