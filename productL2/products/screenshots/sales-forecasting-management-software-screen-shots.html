The Hidden Cost of Disconnected Quote Management

Traditional quoting processes create dangerous gaps between what sales teams promise and what the business can profitably deliver.

Many businesses, from SMEs to large enterprises, struggle to protect profit margins in an era of volatile commodity prices and constantly changing product costs. Traditional quoting processes create dangerous gaps between what sales teams promise and what the business can profitably deliver.

The consequences of disconnected quote management are severe:

  • Quotes sent with inadequate margins due to outdated pricing information
  • Lost profitability when promotions or price changes occur between quote creation and deal closure
  • Friction between sales, production, and quotations departments over conflicting information
  • Time wasted manually gathering data from multiple systems to create accurate quotes
  • Missed sales opportunities due to slow quote turnaround times
  • Difficulty tracking quote status and conversion rates across the sales pipeline
  • Lack of visibility into which products and pricing strategies drive profitability

Most quotation departments operate out of sync with real product costs, current promotions, and actual production capacity. This disconnect means quotes are often created without adequate margins, turning potentially profitable deals into money-losing commitments. A deal may have adequate margin when the quote is created, but inadequate consideration of upcoming price promotions or cost changes can erode profitability by the time the customer accepts.

2

Real-Time Margin Visibility

See instant margin calculations for every line item and overall quote. Visual indicators immediately highlight quotes below target margins, enabling proactive adjustments before quotes are sent to customers, protecting profitability across all deals.

Dynamic Pricing Rules

Configure flexible pricing rules that automatically apply discounts, volume pricing, customer-specific agreements, and promotional offers while enforcing minimum margin thresholds. Rules update in real-time as conditions change, protecting margins automatically.

Analyze past quotes to identify pricing patterns, margin trends, and win/loss factors. Use historical data to optimize pricing strategies, identify products with margin erosion, and coach sales teams on effective pricing tactics that maximize profitability.

Cost Change Alerts

Receive automatic notifications when product costs change between quote creation and customer acceptance. System flags quotes potentially impacted by cost increases, allowing proactive customer communication and margin protection before order confirmation.
