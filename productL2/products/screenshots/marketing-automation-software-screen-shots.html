Control the Lead Qualification Process with Precision

Salesboom offers a dynamic system that gives fine-grained control over how leads are qualified and handed off to sales.

Customizable Lead Scoring

Build sophisticated lead scoring models based on demographic data, behavioral signals, engagement levels, and custom criteria specific to your business. The system automatically scores leads in real-time.

  • Multi-dimensional scoring based on firmographic, demographic, and behavioral data
  • Custom weighting for different actions, content interactions, and engagement patterns
  • Automatic score decay for leads that go dormant
  • Dynamic threshold adjustments based on sales capacity and market conditions
  • A/B testing for scoring models to optimize conversion rates

Intelligent Lead Routing

Once leads reach qualification thresholds, automated routing ensures they reach the right sales representative instantly. The system considers territory, specialization, workload, and performance.

  • Territory-based assignment with automatic geographic detection
  • Round-robin distribution to balance workload across teams
  • Skills-based routing for specialized products or industries
  • Escalation workflows for high-value leads requiring immediate attention
  • Complete audit trails showing lead progression through qualification stages

Sales and Marketing Alignment

Bridge the gap between marketing and sales with transparent qualification criteria, automated handoff workflows, and closed-loop reporting.

  • Reduced lead response times through instant notifications
  • Higher conversion rates from better-qualified opportunities
  • Improved sales productivity by eliminating unqualified leads
  • Data-driven refinement of qualification criteria based on actual outcomes
  • Stronger collaboration through shared metrics and accountability
