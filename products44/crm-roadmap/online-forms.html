The Lead Capture Challenge Every Business Faces

Most businesses struggle with inefficient lead capture processes that waste valuable opportunities. Without intelligent lead capture, you're losing qualified prospects to faster competitors.

Unqualified Leads

Generic forms capture only basic contact information, leaving sales teams blind to lead quality and readiness. Teams waste time calling unqualified prospects who aren't ready to buy.

Manual Data Entry

Manual data entry creates delays, errors, and duplicate records that slow your sales pipeline. Leads land in email inboxes and get manually entered into spreadsheets.

Slow Response Times

Leads go cold while waiting for response. 78% of purchases go to the company that responds first, yet most leads sit for hours or days before anyone follows up.

Poor Routing

Lack of automatic routing means leads reach the wrong person or fall through the cracks entirely. No systematic distribution process leads to chaos and missed opportunities.

No Attribution

No visibility into which marketing channels and websites generate the best leads makes it impossible to optimize marketing spend and strategy effectively.

Lost Opportunities

Sales teams burn time on leads that were never a good fit while qualified prospects slip away to competitors with faster, more responsive processes.

Capture More Than Just Contact Information

Generic name-and-email forms tell you nothing about lead quality or intent. Salesboom's customizable forms let you gather the critical qualification data that determines whether a lead is worth pursuing immediately or needs nurturing.

  • Budget range and purchasing timeline to identify sales-ready prospects
  • Current challenges and pain points to personalize your sales approach
  • Company size, industry, and role to segment and prioritize leads
  • Product interests and specific requirements for targeted follow-up
  • Current solution or competitor being used to tailor competitive positioning
  • Referral source and campaign tracking for marketing attribution
  • Custom qualification questions specific to your sales process

Automatic Routing to the Right Team in Real-Time

Speed matters in lead follow-up. Studies show that companies responding within 5 minutes are 100 times more likely to connect with leads than those waiting 30 minutes. Salesboom automates the entire routing process.

  • Route by geography to assign leads to regional sales representatives automatically
  • Route by company size to direct enterprise leads to senior account executives
  • Route by product interest to ensure product specialists handle technical inquiries
  • Route by lead score to prioritize hot prospects for immediate attention
  • Round-robin distribution to balance workload across your sales team
  • Route by availability to skip team members who are out of office or at capacity
