Salesboom Sandbox: Risk-Free IT Development and Integration

The Salesboom Sandbox is a critical compliance tool that provides a development environment which is an exact copy of your production account. This isolated testing environment is essential for meeting SOX requirements around change management and system integrity.

Safe IT Development Without Risk

Our clients use the Sandbox environment to properly manage IT changes and develop their platform without affecting organizational documentation. Within the Sandbox environment, all IT development can be thoroughly tested before changes are brought into production to ensure the safety of all documentation and financial data.

Key Benefits of the Sandbox:

  • Exact replica of production environment for accurate testing
  • Complete isolation from live financial data
  • Ability to test integrations with current financial systems
  • Risk-free experimentation with workflows and automation
  • Validation of reports and financial controls before deployment
  • Documentation of testing procedures for audit purposes

Seamless Financial System Integration

Salesboom can also provide clients with sandbox capabilities specifically designed for IT development and testing, easing the process of integrating Salesboom with current financial systems like QuickBooks, ERP platforms, and accounting software.

This ensures that financial data flows correctly and compliance controls remain intact during system changes. Test your integrations thoroughly before they touch production data, maintaining the integrity required for SOX compliance.

Bank-Grade Data Security and Protection

Salesboom's data center is housed in a highly secure data warehouse with multiple layers of physical and digital security. All data transmissions are encrypted to banking security standards, ensuring that financial information remains protected during transit and at rest.

Records Management and Document Control

The Salesboom platform is equipped with in-depth Records Management which simplifies storing, managing, and searching documents. Our Records Management System is integrated with our role hierarchy security settings, which are used to allow only those with security access to view and edit any record within the system.

This Integrated Approach Ensures That:

  • Financial documents are properly stored and classified
  • Access is restricted based on role and need-to-know
  • Document retention policies are enforced automatically
  • Records cannot be inappropriately modified or deleted
  • Search and retrieval is fast and audit-compliant
  • Document version history is maintained

Automated Backup and Disaster Recovery

Combined with security controls are Data Backups which are performed nightly to provide extra peace of mind by cloning data and saving it on secure media which is then stored in an off-site fireproof location.

Our Backup Capabilities Include:

  • Nightly automated backups of all data
  • Off-site storage in secure, geographically separate locations
  • Fireproof and disaster-resistant storage facilities
  • Point-in-time recovery capabilities
  • Regular backup validation and testing
  • Rapid restore procedures for business continuity
  • Documentation of backup procedures for audit compliance

This comprehensive backup strategy ensures that financial data is never lost and can be recovered quickly in the event of any system failure, supporting business continuity requirements under SOX.
