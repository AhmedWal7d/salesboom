Common Questions About Salesforce Migration

Get answers to the questions most businesses have when considering migration from Salesforce.

How long does migration take?

Most businesses complete migration in 30-90 minutes for standard configurations. Enterprise accounts with millions of records and complex customizations might take a few hours. The process runs automatically without requiring constant supervision.

Will we lose any data?

No. Migration Magic includes built-in validation that verifies every record transfers correctly. The system flags any discrepancies for immediate review. We also maintain a complete audit trail and can preserve your Salesforce data as a backup during the transition period.

What happens to our Salesforce account?

You maintain control of your Salesforce account. Most businesses keep Salesforce active for a transition period (typically 30-60 days) to ensure everything works perfectly in Salesboom before canceling Salesforce entirely.

Do our users need training?

Because Salesboom is more intuitive than Salesforce, most users require minimal training. We provide comprehensive resources including videos, documentation, and live sessions, but many teams become productive within hours rather than weeks.

Can we customize Salesboom like we did Salesforce?

Yes. Salesboom offers flexible customization including custom fields, objects, workflows, reports, and dashboards. Many businesses discover they can simplify overly complex Salesforce customizations using Salesboom's more elegant approach.

What about our Salesforce integrations?

Migration Magic focuses on core CRM data and processes. Third-party integrations require evaluation and potentially new connections. Salesboom offers native integrations with common tools and an open API for custom connections. Our team helps identify integration requirements and plan accordingly.

Is there a minimum contract?

No. Salesboom operates on month-to-month subscriptions with no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. We believe in earning your business every month through excellent service, not contractual lock-in.

What if we need to go back to Salesforce?

While rare (our retention rate exceeds 95%), you can export your data from Salesboom at any time in standard formats. We don't practice vendor lock-in. That said, most businesses that migrate to Salesboom wonder why they waited so long to make the switch.
