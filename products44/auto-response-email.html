Real-World Automation Examples from Salesboom Customers

Following are some examples of how customers are using our tools to automate their business:

Automated New Lead Response

When a prospect fills out a "Request Demo" form, the system instantly sends a personalized thank-you email with a calendar link, creates a lead record, assigns it to the appropriate sales rep based on territory, and sends an alert with the prospect's website activity history and lead score.

Customer Onboarding Automation

When a deal is marked "Closed Won," workflows automatically send a welcome email sequence, create onboarding tasks for the customer success team, provision user accounts, schedule kickoff calls, and notify relevant departments including finance, operations, and support.

Win-Back Campaign for Inactive Customers

When a customer hasn't placed an order in 90 days, workflows automatically add them to a win-back drip campaign with special offers, success stories from similar customers, and product updates. If engagement remains low, an alert goes to the account manager for personal outreach.

Quote Follow-Up Automation

After sending a quote, the system schedules follow-up reminders at 3 days, 7 days, and 14 days if the prospect hasn't responded. Each reminder includes progressively stronger calls to action and can offer limited-time incentives.

Cross-Sell and Upsell Automation

Based on purchase history and product usage data from integrated systems, workflows identify cross-sell and upsell opportunities and alert account managers when customers are ready for expansion conversations. Auto-responders can send educational content about complementary products.

Contract Renewal Management

90 days before contract expiration, workflows trigger renewal campaigns including usage reports, ROI calculations, testimonials, and upgrade options. Alerts notify account managers when renewal conversations should begin, ensuring no contract expires due to oversight.
