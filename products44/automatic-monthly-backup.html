Modern businesses need proactive, automated protection that works consistently without human intervention.

Enterprise-Grade Backup Technology

Salesboom's backup infrastructure leverages enterprise-class technology typically reserved for Fortune 500 companies, now accessible to businesses of all sizes through our platform.

Incremental Backup Methodology

While full backups run monthly, incremental changes are captured continuously, minimizing data loss windows. This hybrid approach balances comprehensive protection with storage efficiency and rapid recovery capabilities.

Multi-Tier Storage Strategy

Recent backups are maintained on high-performance SSD storage for rapid restoration. Older archives move to cost-efficient long-term storage while remaining instantly accessible when needed.

Geographic Redundancy

Your backup data is replicated across multiple data centers in different geographic regions. If one location experiences an outage or disaster, your backup remains accessible from alternative sites.

AES-256 Encryption

All backup data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. Only authorized Salesboom systems can decrypt your backup data, ensuring protection from unauthorized access.

Extended Retention Policy

Monthly backups are retained for extended periods, providing the ability to restore historical data if needed. This extended retention supports compliance requirements, legal discovery requests, and recovery from long-undetected corruption issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Automatic Monthly Backup and data recovery procedures.

How do I restore lost data?

Simply contact our support team. We'll work with you to identify the appropriate backup version and restoration scope, then execute the recovery process. Most restorations complete within 24 hours.

Can I access backup data myself?

Backup restoration requires Salesboom team involvement to ensure data integrity and prevent accidental overwrites. You can request backup verification reports for compliance or audit purposes.

How far back can I restore data?

We retain monthly backups according to our retention policy, typically providing the ability to restore data from several months in the past. This protects against slow-developing corruption issues.

Is backup included in my subscription?

Yes! Automatic Monthly Backup is included as a standard feature for all Salesboom CRM subscribers at no additional cost. We believe data protection is fundamental to responsible CRM service delivery.

What about daily or weekly backups?

While full monthly backups are standard, incremental changes are captured more frequently to minimize potential data loss windows. For enterprise customers, we can discuss more frequent backup schedules.

How do you ensure backup security?

All backup data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption. Backups are stored in geographically distributed data centers with physical security controls. Only authorized Salesboom systems can decrypt backup data.

Can I get a copy of my backup?

We can provide data exports in standard formats (CSV, JSON, XML) for your independent archival purposes. These exports contain your business data in accessible formats you can store according to your policies.

What if backup fails?

Our backup system includes automated monitoring and verification. If a backup fails or encounters errors, our technical team is alerted immediately and takes corrective action before the next backup cycle.
