The High Cost of Missed Opportunities

In the fast-paced world of sales, big deals require immediate attention and coordinated team effort. Yet many organizations struggle with a critical challenge: high-value opportunities languishing in the pipeline without appropriate visibility or urgency.

The Consequences Are Severe:

  • Sales reps juggle dozens of deals and miss critical follow-up windows on major opportunities
  • Leadership discovers important deals too late to provide strategic guidance or resources
  • Competitors move faster while your team remains unaware of deal urgency
  • Revenue forecasts become unreliable without real-time visibility into pipeline health
  • Deals stall or die due to lack of executive involvement at crucial moments
  • Sales managers waste time manually checking pipelines instead of coaching
The Reality

Without automated alerting, your biggest revenue opportunities receive the same attention as routine deals—or worse, get forgotten entirely until it's too late to recover them.

Intelligent Threshold Configuration

Every organization defines "big deal" differently based on their business model, industry, and revenue targets. Salesboom's Big Deal Alert adapts to your unique requirements with fully customizable threshold settings.

Configure Alerts Based On:

  • Opportunity Amount: Revenue value thresholds
  • Probability Percentage: Likelihood of closing
  • Combined Scoring: Weighs both amount and probability factors
  • Deal Stage: Milestone progression triggers
  • Customer Type: Account tier or classification
  • Product Category: Service or product line
  • Geographic Region: Territory-based criteria
  • Time Windows: Closing date urgency
Example Configuration

You might set alerts to trigger when an opportunity exceeds $100,000 with a closing probability above 60%, or when any deal over $50,000 reaches the proposal stage. The system supports multiple threshold configurations to accommodate different deal types, products, or business units.

Deploy Big Deal Alerts in Days, Not Months

Salesboom's Big Deal Alert feature is included with all CRM subscriptions at no additional cost. Organizations can configure and deploy alerts within days using our straightforward setup process.

Week 1: Planning

Work with your sales leadership team to define what constitutes a "big deal" for your organization. Consider historical data, revenue targets, and sales capacity. Identify who should receive alerts based on deal characteristics and organizational structure. Document your initial threshold settings and notification routing rules.

Week 2: Configuration

Access Salesboom's Big Deal Alert configuration panel and enter your threshold criteria. Set up notification recipients and routing rules. Customize alert content and formatting. Run test alerts to ensure proper delivery and formatting. Review and refine based on test results.

Week 3: Launch and Monitor

Activate Big Deal Alerts and communicate the launch to your sales organization. Explain the purpose, benefits, and expected response protocols. Monitor alert volume and team engagement closely during the first week. Gather feedback from alert recipients and sales team members.

Week 4: Optimize

Review analytics from the first two weeks of operation. Adjust thresholds if alert volume is too high or too low. Refine notification recipients based on engagement patterns. Update alert content based on team feedback. Document initial results and plan quarterly review schedule.
