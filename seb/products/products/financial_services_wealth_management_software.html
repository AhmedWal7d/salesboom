Comprehensive Portfolio Tracking and Household Management

Financial advisors require specialized tools to manage the complexity of modern wealth management. Salesboom's CRM delivers sophisticated portfolio tracking and household management capabilities.

Portfolio Tracking and Consolidated Views

The system provides consolidated book of business tracking at both the household level and individual contact level. Advisors can view complete portfolio information, track investment performance, monitor asset allocation, and access integrated market data services—all within a single platform.

This consolidated view enables advisors to quickly understand client positions and identify portfolio gaps that represent cross-sell opportunities.

Household Management and Family Relationships

Wealth management often involves entire families, not just individual clients. Our household management capabilities provide a single household view that tracks financial and investment risk profiles across all family members.

Advisors can see consolidated portfolios, leads, opportunities, notes, and tasks for all contacts in the household, enabling them to identify family-wide planning opportunities and deliver coordinated service across generations.

Consolidated Household Profiles

The CRM creates consolidated household profiles that link related contacts, accounts, and portfolios. This enables advisors to understand total household wealth, track multi-generational relationships, identify estate planning opportunities, and deliver family-focused financial strategies.

These capabilities increase overall wallet share and strengthen multi-generational loyalty across your client base.
