Introducing Promptuit AMS

The Virtual AI Sales Computer That Integrates Seamlessly With Your CRM

What is Promptuit AMS?

Promptuit AMS is a dedicated AI workspace that works alongside your CRM, not against it. Think of it as a virtual sales computer where AI agents can operate safely, generate content, and collaborate autonomously while your sales reps maintain complete control.

Built by Salesboom with 20+ years of CRM expertise, Promptuit integrates seamlessly with CRM, ERP, QuickBooks, and Outlook365 to create a powerful augmentation engine for your sales team.

Safety-First CRM AI Integration

A two-system design that protects your data while supercharging productivity

1

Read-Only CRM Access

AI agents can read your CRM data but cannot change it. All work stays in the AMS until a human reviews and approves it.

2

AI Sandbox Environment

Agents generate content, proposals, and analysis in a safe workspace. Errors are quarantined, never reaching your production CRM.

3

Human-in-the-Loop

Salespeople remain the final decision-makers. Review, edit, and approve all AI outputs before they sync to your CRM.

Data Integrity Guaranteed

No corrupted records. No unauthorized changes. Your CRM stays clean while AI handles the heavy lifting.
