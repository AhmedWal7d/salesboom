The All-in-One Solution That Eliminates Data Silos

Salesboom delivers a comprehensive cloud-based platform that unifies your organization's strategic sales, marketing, and customer service operations into a single, user-friendly system.

Unlike fragmented solutions that force you to manage multiple vendors and disconnected data, Salesboom provides a true virtual command center for your entire business.

The platform's integrated architecture breaks down departmental silos by providing instant company-wide access to complete customer histories, interactions, complaints, and purchases. Sales, marketing, and service teams work from the same centralized database, ensuring everyone has the information they need to deliver exceptional customer experiences.

With over 22 years of proven innovation and 3,500+ businesses across 159 countries trusting the platform, Salesboom has established itself as a reliable partner for organizations seeking to streamline processes, enhance collaboration, and make data-driven decisions that drive measurable growth.

Simplify Sales Compensation with Centralized Commission Tracking

Salesboom provides a robust, centralized solution for commissions and compensation management—a critical capability for sales-driven organizations.

Managers can flawlessly monitor all commissions earned and manage multiple commission plans simultaneously, whether for inside sales representatives, outside sales teams, channel partners, or hybrid structures. The system handles the complexity so you can focus on motivating and rewarding your team.

Calculate extra earnings, compensations, and bonuses with precision and speed. Instantly identify your top earners at any point in a sales cycle or marketing campaign, enabling recognition and strategic resource allocation based on actual performance data.

Sales agents can request commissions on closed-won deals directly through the system, while managers calculate and distribute payments with confidence in the accuracy of every dollar. Real-time commission reports provide complete visibility into total commissions earned by employee, team, or time period.

The platform's integrated QuickBooks connection streamlines the entire process from calculation to payment distribution, though it can also operate as a standalone solution for businesses with different accounting systems.
