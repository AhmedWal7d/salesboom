From One Video to Ten Marketing Assets—Automatically

Content creation is the bottleneck that kills marketing momentum. Demandinator solves this through intelligent automation that turns every piece of content into a multi-channel campaign.

The "Loom-to-Lead" Pipeline

Step 1: Create Once

Record a single 5-minute video using Loom—explaining a concept, sharing a customer win, or addressing a common objection. No studio, no script, just authentic expertise.

Step 2: AI Processing

Demandinator automatically sends your video to OpusClip's AI, which analyzes the content and creates multiple short-form clips optimized for different platforms. Each clip receives a "virality score" based on hook strength, pacing, and engagement patterns.

Step 3: Content Generation

GPT-4 integration analyzes your video transcript and automatically generates blog posts, email sequences, LinkedIn posts, and Twitter threads—all maintaining your authentic voice and messaging.

Step 4: Distribution

Content is automatically distributed across channels: Video clips to YouTube and social media, blog post published to your website, email sequences loaded into nurture campaigns, LinkedIn posts scheduled throughout the week.

Result: One 5-minute video becomes 10+ marketing assets distributed across every channel—all without manual editing, writing, or scheduling. You create once, the AI multiplies, and Demandinator orchestrates.
