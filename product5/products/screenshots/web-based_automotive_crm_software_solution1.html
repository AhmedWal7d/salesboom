Salesboom Automotive CRM software systems are designed to meet the most demanding requirements for automotive sales, marketing, call centers, service centers, and field operations. Comprehensive Dealership Management CRM Solutions

End-to-end dealership management tools that connect every aspect of your operation.

Vehicle Sales Tracking

Track every deal from initial prospect contact through vehicle delivery. The system captures complete deal information including vehicle details, trade-in valuations, financing options, and final sale terms. Monitor sales pipeline progress, forecast revenue, and identify bottlenecks that slow deal closure.

Finance & Insurance Calculations

Integrated F&I tools calculate payment options, interest rates, loan terms, and insurance products in real-time during the sales process. Present multiple financing scenarios to customers instantly, compare lender options, and maximize F&I revenue per vehicle sold.

Lead Management & Follow-Up

Capture sales leads from all touchpoints including showroom visits, website inquiries, phone calls, and marketing campaigns. Automatically or manually assign leads to sales representatives based on territories, specialties, or workload. Track all customer interactions and follow-up activities.

Commission Calculations

Automatically calculate sales commissions based on configurable rules for vehicle sales, F&I products, and service contracts. Track individual and team performance, generate commission reports, and integrate with payroll systems. Support complex commission structures including bonuses and spiffs.

Inventory Management

Search and allocate vehicles from inventory relative to customer requirements. Track vehicle location, age, cost, and pricing history. Set automated alerts for aging inventory that requires price adjustments or special promotions. Integrate with manufacturer ordering systems.

Customer Self-Service Portal

Enable customers to browse inventory, schedule test drives, apply for financing, track their deal status, and schedule service appointments through a branded web portal. Reduce phone calls and administrative work while improving customer convenience.

Automotive Parts CRM and Inventory Management

Specialized tools to manage complex inventory, supplier relationships, and wholesale and retail customers.

Comprehensive Inventory Control

Manage parts inventory with complete SKU tracking, stock levels, reorder points, and supplier information. Support multiple warehouses and distribution points with real-time visibility across all locations. Track parts movement from distribution center to retail stores, mitigating distance and communication challenges.

Purchase Order Management

Generate and manage purchase orders from multiple suppliers. Track order status, expected delivery dates, and partial shipments. Automatically update inventory levels upon receipt. Compare supplier pricing and lead times to optimize purchasing decisions.

Work Order Integration

Link parts directly to service work orders for accurate job costing and inventory depletion. Track parts usage by technician, service type, and vehicle model. Demand Forecasting

Analyze historical sales data and service patterns to predict future parts demand. Optimize inventory levels to prevent stockouts while minimizing carrying costs. Identify seasonal trends and adjust purchasing accordingly. Support data-driven inventory investment decisions.
