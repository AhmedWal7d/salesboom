Powerful CRM and Accounting Integration for Modern Businesses

Salesboom Cloud CRM is fully integrated with Xero, the leading cloud accounting software for small businesses.

This powerful combination creates a unified system that synchronizes customer data in real-time between the CRM and accounting databases, eliminating duplicate data entry and reducing errors.

With Cloud CRM integrated with accounting in the cloud, even small businesses can afford an enterprise-grade system to manage their entire operation with just one or two logins. No more switching between disconnected systems or manually transferring information between platforms.

Stay in your user interface of choice and keep sales quotations, timesheets, and invoicing tightly integrated between the CRM and accounting systems. Whether your team prefers working in Salesboom or Xero, data flows seamlessly between both platforms automatically.

Xero provides online accounting software that manages invoicing, bank reconciliation, bookkeeping, and more. When combined with Salesboom's comprehensive CRM capabilities, businesses gain complete visibility across sales, service, and financial operations.

Real-Time Data Synchronization Across Your Business

The Salesboom-Xero integration delivers powerful capabilities that streamline operations and improve efficiency across your organization.

Unified Customer Data Management

Update contact information for contacts and accounts in the CRM and have customer data automatically synchronized between Salesboom and Xero. Changes made in either system are reflected instantly, ensuring your teams always work with accurate, up-to-date information. No more data discrepancies or duplicate records.

Seamless Quote Generation and Management

Generate quotes in Salesboom or in Xero as you prefer. The quotes are automatically replicated from the CRM to Xero and vice versa, giving both sales and accounting teams immediate visibility. Sales teams can create professional quotes in the CRM, while accounting can access them directly in Xero for invoicing and financial tracking.

Integrated Project Tracking and Timesheets

Track projects and timesheets in Salesboom Cloud CRM and/or in Xero. The timesheets and invoices are kept synchronized between both systems, ensuring accurate project costing and billing. Consultants and service professionals can log time in their preferred interface while accounting maintains perfect accuracy for invoicing and profitability analysis.

Automated Invoice Management

Invoices created in either system are automatically synchronized, maintaining consistency between sales records and financial records. This eliminates manual data entry, reduces billing errors, and accelerates the quote-to-cash cycle. Your accounting team always has real-time visibility into sales activities and revenue.
