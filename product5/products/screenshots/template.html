Powerful Automation Features That Work for You

The Salesboom Workflow Automation Engine offers comprehensive automation capabilities designed to handle your most time-consuming business processes.

Trigger-Based Automation

Create sophisticated workflows that activate automatically when records are created or updated. Set multiple field criteria and values to ensure workflows trigger precisely when needed. This eliminates manual monitoring and ensures consistent execution of business rules.

Intelligent Task Assignment

Automatically route tasks to the appropriate user based on predefined rules, workload, expertise, or availability. The system ensures work is distributed efficiently and nothing gets lost or forgotten. Tasks include complete context so users can take immediate action.

Automated Email Alerts

Send instant notifications using customizable email templates when important events occur. Keep managers, team members, and stakeholders informed without manual follow-up. Templates can be personalized with dynamic fields pulled directly from your CRM data.

Multi-Criteria Rule Engine

Build complex automation rules using any combination of field values, thresholds, time periods, and conditions. The flexible rule engine adapts to your unique business processes without requiring custom coding or IT involvement.

Escalation Management

Automatically escalate issues based on age, severity, customer value, or any custom criteria. Ensure critical situations receive immediate attention from the right people. Define escalation paths with multiple levels to guarantee resolution.

Real-Time Execution

Workflows trigger instantly when conditions are met, not on delayed batch schedules. This ensures time-sensitive issues receive immediate attention and opportunities aren't missed due to processing delays.

Supercharge Your Sales Team with Intelligent Automation

Transform sales operations by automating routine tasks and ensuring managers stay informed about critical opportunities.

Big Deal Alerts

Automatically alert sales managers when an opportunity reaches a specified threshold based on deal amount and probability. This ensures high-value deals receive appropriate attention and resources at critical moments. Managers can intervene proactively to help close important opportunities without constantly monitoring the pipeline.

Opportunity Downgrade Alerts

Trigger immediate notifications when an opportunity gets downgraded below a certain threshold based on the deal amount and/or probability. Early warning enables managers to understand why deals are slipping and take corrective action before opportunities are lost completely.

Lead Follow-Up Automation

Alert designated users when a lead has not been contacted for more than a set number of days. This prevents leads from going cold due to oversight or workload issues. Automatic reminders ensure consistent follow-up and maximize conversion rates without manual tracking.

Stale Opportunity Detection

Identify opportunities that haven't been updated in a specified timeframe and automatically notify the sales manager. This prevents deals from languishing unattended and ensures the pipeline remains active and current.

Elevate Customer Service with Automated Case Management

Ensure every customer issue receives timely attention through intelligent escalation and routing.

Case Age Escalation

Automatically escalate support cases that have been open beyond a defined threshold to ensure timely resolution. Prevent customer frustration by guaranteeing that older issues receive priority attention from senior support staff or management.

VIP Customer Routing

Instantly route cases from high-value customers to specialized support teams or senior agents. Ensure your most important customers receive premium service levels automatically based on account value, contract tier, or custom criteria.

Priority Issue Alerts

Trigger immediate notifications when cases are marked with high priority or critical severity. Ensure urgent issues bypass normal queues and reach the appropriate resolver immediately, minimizing customer downtime and impact.

Follow-Up Reminders

Schedule automatic follow-up tasks and reminders for support agents to check in with customers after case resolution. Improve satisfaction scores and catch issues that weren't fully resolved on the first attempt.

Quick Deployment with Ongoing Flexibility

Implementing workflow automation doesn't require lengthy IT projects or complex change management.

1

Rapid Setup

Most workflow rules can be configured and activated in minutes, not weeks. The intuitive interface guides you through the setup process with clear options and real-time validation. Start seeing benefits immediately rather than waiting months for custom development.

2

Template-Based Deployment

Leverage pre-built workflow templates based on industry best practices. Salesboom has worked with thousands of companies across 159 countries, and that collective wisdom is built into ready-to-use templates. Customize as needed or deploy as-is.

3

Test Before Activating

Test workflows in a sandbox environment before making them live. Verify that rules trigger correctly, emails render properly, and tasks route to the right users. Catch issues before they impact operations.

4

Iterative Refinement

Start with basic automation and add complexity over time. Begin with simple alert rules, then graduate to multi-step workflows as your team becomes comfortable. There's no requirement to automate everything at once—build incrementally.

5

User Permissions

Control who can create, modify, and deactivate workflows through role-based permissions. Ensure business rules remain consistent while empowering appropriate users to make changes. Audit logs track all workflow modifications for compliance and troubleshooting.

6

Ongoing Monitoring

Workflow execution data feeds into analytics dashboards, providing visibility into automation performance. Track how many workflows trigger, which rules fire most frequently, time saved through automation, and opportunities for further optimization.
