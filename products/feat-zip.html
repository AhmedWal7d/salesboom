The Hidden Cost of Missed Opportunities

Field sales representatives, account managers, and business development professionals spend thousands of dollars traveling to trade shows, conferences, client meetings, and regional events. Yet most return home having only met with scheduled appointments, completely unaware of the prospects, existing customers, and hot opportunities located just minutes away from their hotel.

The challenges facing mobile sales teams:

  • No visibility into which customers and prospects are nearby during business travel
  • Manual spreadsheet searches that waste hours and miss critical connections
  • Lost revenue from nearby opportunities that remain undiscovered
  • Inefficient territory planning that fails to maximize geographic proximity
  • Inability to quickly pivot and fill cancelled appointments with nearby meetings
  • Poor coordination between marketing events and local sales opportunities

Traditional CRM systems force sales teams to manually search through records, export to spreadsheets, or use separate mapping tools that don't integrate with customer data. This fragmented approach means money left on the table and relationships left unbuilt.
