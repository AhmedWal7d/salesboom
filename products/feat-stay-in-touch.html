The Hidden Cost of Outdated Contact Information

Every business depends on accurate contact information to reach customers, prospects, and partners. Yet maintaining current records remains one of the most challenging and time-consuming tasks organizations face.

People change jobs, phone numbers, email addresses, and locations constantly, creating a silent erosion of your most valuable business asset—your contact database.

The Consequences Are Significant:

  • Wasted marketing budgets on emails that bounce or reach the wrong recipients
  • Lost sales opportunities when you cannot reach interested prospects
  • Damaged brand reputation from communications sent to incorrect addresses
  • Reduced customer satisfaction when important updates never arrive
  • Compliance risks from maintaining inaccurate personal data
  • Decreased team productivity spent manually updating records one by one
The Challenge

The constant exchange of information and movement of people in today's dynamic business environment makes manual contact maintenance virtually impossible. You need an automated solution that keeps your database current without consuming valuable staff time.

Automated Contact Verification That Works While You Focus on Business

Salesboom's Stay-in-Touch Mass Email tool transforms the challenging task of contact database maintenance into a simple, automated process.

This time-saving feature enables your sales staff, managers, and team members to mass email selected leads and contacts using intelligent mail-merge templates that request recipients to review and confirm their information.

The System Works Seamlessly:

  • Select the contacts or leads you want to verify from your CRM database

  • The tool automatically mail-merges personalized contact information into each email

This automated approach ensures you maintain accurate, up-to-date contact information across your entire database with minimal effort, maximizing your communication effectiveness and strengthening customer relations.

Powerful Contact Management Features That Save Hours Daily

The Stay-in-Touch Mass Email tool delivers comprehensive functionality designed to keep your contact database pristine and your customer relationships strong.

Intelligent Mail Merge

Automatically personalizes each verification email with the recipient's specific contact information. The mail-merge engine pulls data directly from your CRM, including name, title, company, phone numbers, email addresses, and mailing addresses. Recipients see their information formatted professionally, making it easy to spot errors and provide corrections.

Automatic History Tracking

Every Stay-in-Touch email sent is automatically logged in the contact's activity history within the CRM. This creates a complete audit trail showing when verification requests were sent, which contacts responded, what changes were made, and when follow-ups are needed. Managers gain visibility into contact database health without requesting reports.

Selective Contact Targeting

Send verification emails strategically to specific contact segments rather than blasting your entire database. Filter by last update date, contact status, customer tier, geographic region, or any custom criteria. This targeted approach reduces email fatigue while focusing verification efforts where they're most needed.

Template Customization

Customize the Stay-in-Touch email template to match your brand voice and specific verification needs. Add your logo, adjust messaging, include additional fields for verification, and personalize the call-to-action. Professional templates ensure recipients take your verification requests seriously.

Response Management

When contacts respond with updated information, the system makes it simple to review changes and update records. Responses are linked directly to the original contact record, eliminating the need to search for where information belongs. Bulk approval options speed up processing for large verification campaigns.
