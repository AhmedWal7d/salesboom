What Is the Agent Management System?

The world's first Cloud CRM built specifically for AI Agents

A Sales AI Computer Built for Safety & Productivity

The Agent Management System (AMS), also known as Promptuit, is a revolutionary platform created by Salesboom, a Halifax-based pioneer in Cloud CRM since 2003. While traditional CRMs manage human sales users, the AMS is designed to safely manage, train, and deploy semi-autonomous AI agents that work alongside your sales teams.

By leveraging 23+ years of CRM expertise, Salesboom has created the only system that ensures AI agents can work productively without putting your sensitive business data at risk.

What Makes AMS Different?

  • Sandbox environment for safe AI operations
  • Context management reduces errors
  • Human-in-the-loop oversight
  • Seamless CRM/ERP integration
  • Built-to-Suit customization

Safety & Trust Built-In

AI agents can go "off the rails" without proper controls. We designed AMS with safety at its core.

Read-Only CRM Access

AI agents can never overwrite or delete customer records. All work is reviewed by humans before syncing to your CRM.

Human-in-the-Loop

Every AI-generated item requires human approval before publication. You maintain complete control over what goes live.

Complete Audit Trails

Track every AI action, decision, and approval. Full accountability and compliance for regulated industries.
