Complete CRM Solution: All the Modules Your Business Needs

Salesboom provides a comprehensive, integrated platform that combines customer relationship management and sales force automation in one unified system.

Home - Command Center

Your centralized dashboard for calendar management and task operations. View all your appointments, deadlines, and to-do items in one place with automatic reminders.

Leads - Capture Every Opportunity

Track web leads, mailing lists, and sales visits in one system. Automatically capture leads from your website, assign to team members, and measure conversion rates.

Accounts - Client Relationship Hub

Manage both existing clients and potential clients with complete account profiles. Store company information, interaction history, and purchase records all in one place.

Contacts - Decision Maker Intelligence

Track additional influencers and decision makers within each account. Build relationships with all the right people to strengthen account penetration.

Opportunities - Pipeline Management

Monitor business possibilities and opportunities from existing clients. Track deal stages, probability, expected close dates, and revenue potential through your sales pipeline.

Forecasts - Revenue Visibility

Generate quarterly revenue projections based on your opportunities. Make informed business decisions with accurate forecasting for planning hiring, inventory, and growth.

Cases - Support Excellence

Handle customer queries, support calls, and training issues efficiently. Log every interaction, track response times, and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

Solutions - Knowledge Base

Access historical data on past issues that may be recurring. Build an institutional knowledge base that captures solutions to common problems for faster resolution.

Reports - Data-Driven Insights

Each module includes preconceived reports giving instant visibility into business performance. Analyze sales trends, customer service metrics, and team productivity.
