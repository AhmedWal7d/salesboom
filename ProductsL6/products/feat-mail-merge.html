The Document Generation Challenge for Growing Businesses

Sales and marketing teams waste countless hours manually copying customer data from CRM systems into contracts, proposals, reports, and email campaigns. This tedious process creates multiple pain points that slow down business operations and reduce team productivity.

The consequences of manual document generation include:

  • Hours wasted on repetitive copy-paste tasks that could be automated
  • Inconsistent formatting and frequent data entry errors that damage credibility
  • Missed opportunities due to slow turnaround times on proposals and contracts
  • Difficulty scaling marketing campaigns because of manual processes
  • Frustrated team members stuck doing administrative work instead of selling
  • Data accuracy issues when information becomes outdated between CRM and documents

Modern businesses need a solution that bridges the gap between CRM intelligence and document creation, allowing teams to generate professional, data-accurate documents in seconds rather than hours.

Effortless Template Creation With Intelligent Tag Generation

Creating mail merge templates is simple and intuitive thanks to Salesboom's built-in Tag Generator tool.

This powerful feature eliminates guesswork and technical complexity, allowing anyone on your team to create professional merge templates in minutes.

How the Tag Generator works:

  • Select the CRM record type you want to merge (contacts, accounts, opportunities, custom objects)
  • Choose from any available field in that record type (name, company, address, phone, custom fields)
  • The system automatically generates the correct Salesboom merge tag with proper syntax
  • Copy and paste the generated tag into your Word, Excel, or email template
  • When you execute the mail merge, tags are instantly replaced with real data from corresponding records

The Tag Generator supports all standard and custom fields in your CRM, giving you unlimited flexibility in template design. Create templates for any business scenariofrom simple contact letters to complex multi-page proposals with pricing tables, product specifications, and customer histories.

No technical knowledge required. No coding. No complex formulas. Just point, click, and create powerful automated documents that pull live data from your CRM every time you use them.
