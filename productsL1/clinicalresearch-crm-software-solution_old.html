Navigating the Complex Landscape of Pharmaceutical and Clinical Research

The pharmaceutical and clinical research industry faces unprecedented challenges that demand sophisticated technology solutions. Patent cliffs threaten revenue streams, increased regulations create compliance complexity, and pricing pressures squeeze margins from every angle. These transformations force organizations to accomplish more with fewer resources while educating clients more effectively than ever before.

The Consequences of Outdated Systems:

  • Fragmented data across research teams, investigators, and administrative functions
  • Delayed information sharing that slows critical decision-making
  • Costly errors in clinical trial management and regulatory submissions
  • Inefficient investigator relationship management hampering recruitment
  • Poor visibility into trial status and research associate activities
  • Difficulty adapting sales and marketing models to rapidly changing market conditions

The Solution

Without integrated technology solutions, pharmaceutical enterprises and clinical research organizations struggle to compete effectively. Research teams waste valuable time on manual processes instead of advancing scientific discovery. Administrative bottlenecks delay time-to-market for breakthrough treatments.

The solution lies in cloud-based CRM technology specifically designed for the unique demands of life sciences organizations.

Cloud CRM Built Specifically for Life Sciences

Salesboom delivers cloud-based CRM solutions engineered specifically for the pharmaceutical and clinical research industries. Since 2003, we have enabled life sciences organizations with technology that empowers rather than limits their capabilities.

Our platform provides comprehensive front-end and back-end integration for efficient end-to-end management of order and supply activities, investigator relationships, research associate results, and clinical trial status tracking.

Enabling Pharmaceutical Representatives Anywhere

Pharmaceutical representatives face intense competition to secure access to physician offices and hospitals. Success requires building strong relationships and staying competitive through superior information management and responsiveness.

Salesboom enables pharmaceutical reps to access the complete CRM solution anywhere through the Internet. Representatives can review physician interaction history, update call notes, check sample inventory, and coordinate activities in real-time from any location. Managers gain instant visibility into field activities through robust reporting, allowing them to identify opportunities, address challenges, and optimize territory performance without waiting for end-of-week reports.

The cloud-based architecture eliminates the frustration of outdated desktop software and provides the flexibility demanded by today's mobile workforce.

Comprehensive Pipeline and Process Management

The platform manages the complete marketing pipeline, sales processes, and lead generation activities while automating routine marketing tasks for pharmaceutical companies of any size. Our scalable, flexible solution serves over 1,500 businesses worldwide across 159 countries.

Key capabilities include:

  • Lead management tools that help representatives build lasting relationships with healthcare providers
  • Automated lead scoring and prioritization based on prescribing patterns and engagement
  • Marketing campaign tracking from initial outreach through prescription conversion
  • Sample management with inventory tracking and compliance documentation
  • Territory optimization based on prescriber value and competitive intelligence
  • Integration with existing ERP systems for seamless order and supply chain management

Streamlining Complex Clinical Research Operations

Clinical research organizations require sophisticated tools to manage the intricate interactions between physician investigators, research teams, and administrative functions. Salesboom delivers stable Cloud CRM solutions that integrate and streamline these complex relationships throughout the clinical research process and the critical transition from investigation to prescribing.

Enhanced Information Sharing

The CRM tracks and enhances information sharing for team members in an on-demand cloud environment. Research coordinators, investigators, sponsors, and administrative staff access the same real-time data, eliminating version control issues and communication gaps. The result is improved communications, timely response to critical information, and better efficiency without costly delays or errors in support of clinical research activities.

Clinical Trial Status Tracking

Monitor every aspect of clinical trials in real-time with comprehensive dashboards and reporting. Track subject enrollment against targets, monitor protocol adherence, document adverse events, and manage study milestones. Automated alerts notify team members of critical deadlines, required actions, and potential compliance issues before they become problems.

Investigator Relationship Management

Build and maintain strong relationships with physician investigators through complete interaction history, communication tracking, and performance analytics. The system facilitates investigator recruitment, qualification, training documentation, and ongoing performance monitoring. Identify top-performing sites, address underperforming locations, and optimize site selection for future studies based on historical data.

Research Associate Activity Tracking

Monitor research associate activities, site visits, and deliverables in real-time. Document training completion, track time allocation across multiple studies, and ensure consistent quality standards. Built-in workflow automation ensures proper documentation and follow-up on every site interaction.

Security and Regulatory Compliance Built-In

Protecting sensitive clinical research data while maintaining regulatory compliance is paramount. Salesboom Cloud CRM delivers enterprise-grade security and compliance features designed specifically for the life sciences industry.

Multi-Layer Security

Comprehensive security architecture protects sensitive data at every level with role-based access controls, encrypted data transmission, secure data storage, and multi-factor authentication options for enhanced protection.

Complete Audit Trails

Every system action is logged with comprehensive audit trails that track user access, data modifications, and system activities. Meet regulatory requirements with tamper-proof logs and detailed activity reporting.

Regulatory Compliance

Built-in compliance features support adherence to FDA 21 CFR Part 11, GCP (Good Clinical Practice), HIPAA, and international regulatory requirements for clinical research operations.

Data Protection

Automated backup procedures, disaster recovery capabilities, data retention policies, and secure data deletion protocols ensure your research data is protected throughout its lifecycle.

Access Management

Granular permission controls ensure team members access only the information they need. Hierarchical permission structures, time-based access controls, and session management maintain security integrity.

Electronic Signatures

Electronic signature capabilities with complete audit trails meet regulatory requirements while streamlining approval workflows and document management processes.
