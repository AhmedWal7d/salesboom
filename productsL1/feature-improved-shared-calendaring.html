Salesboom CRM's shared calendaring puts complete schedule coordination at your fingertips. Create unlimited calendars and share them flexibly with individual users or entire groups, all while maintaining precise control over who sees what.

Unlike basic calendar tools, Salesboom provides enterprise-grade permission controls that balance transparency with privacy. Whether you need to show full event details to your executive team or simply display availability to external partners, you control exactly what each person can see and do.

The system integrates directly into your CRM workflow, connecting calendar activities with opportunities, accounts, and contacts. This means every scheduled meeting carries complete business context, eliminating the need to switch between systems or search for customer information.

Five Levels of Calendar Access for Complete Control

Granular permissions that balance transparency with privacy

1

Hide Details

Users see only availabilitywhether time slots are free or busywithout viewing any event details. Perfect for sharing availability with external partners, vendors, or clients who need to schedule meetings but shouldn't see confidential business activities.

2

Hide Details and Add Events

Users can see availability and add new events to the calendar, but cannot view existing event details. Ideal for reception staff or assistants who schedule appointments but don't need access to sensitive meeting information.

3

Show Details

Users can view complete event details including attendees, locations, and notes, but cannot add new events or modify existing ones. Great for team members who need awareness of activities but shouldn't make scheduling changes.

4

Show Details and Add Events

Users can view all event details and create new events, but cannot edit or delete existing events. Perfect for team members who need to schedule their own activities while maintaining visibility into team schedules.

5

Full Access

Users can view all event details, create new events, and edit or delete existing events. Reserved for managers, executive assistants, and trusted team members who need complete calendar management capabilities.
