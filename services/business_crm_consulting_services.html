Building Your CRM Strategy: Our Proven Methodology

Effective CRM strategy development starts with deep understanding, not software deployment. Our consultants follow a proven process that ensures successful implementation and lasting results.

1 Business Process Discovery

We begin by understanding your current small business processes from end to end. Our consultants work with your team to map how customer interactions flow through your organization, identifying bottlenecks, redundancies, and opportunities for improvement. This discovery phase ensures we understand your unique business model before making any recommendations.

2 Workflow Definition and Optimization

Once processes are understood, we define optimized workflows for your entire small business operation. This includes sales pipelines, customer service protocols, marketing automation sequences, and cross-department handoffs. We document these workflows clearly, creating a blueprint for how your CRM will support and enhance your operations.

3 Custom CRM Configuration

With workflows defined, Salesboom's CRM consultants tailor-fit our hosted CRM solution to your specific small business needs. This includes customizing fields, creating automated workflows, setting up dashboards, configuring reports, and establishing user permissions. The result is a CRM system that feels like it was built specifically for your business—because it was.

4 Employee Education and Change Management

Technology only succeeds when people embrace it. Our consultants educate your employees on the specific benefits of utilizing CRM solutions—how it will ease their workflow, increase their productivity, and help them serve customers better. We address concerns, demonstrate value, and build enthusiasm for the new system.
