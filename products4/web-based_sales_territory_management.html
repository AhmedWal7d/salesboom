Intelligent Lead Routing and Assignment

Salesboom's automated lead routing engine ensures every opportunity reaches the right sales representative instantly. Whether your territories are based on geography, industry vertical, company size, product line, or any combination of variables, our flexible rule engine adapts to your exact business requirements.

  • Configure unlimited territory rules based on any data field or combination of criteria
  • Automatic assignment of incoming leads to appropriate territories in real-time
  • Round-robin distribution within territories to balance workload fairly
  • Priority routing for high-value prospects based on lead scoring
  • Overflow rules that reassign leads when representatives reach capacity
  • Geographic boundaries with ZIP code, city, state, or custom region mapping
  • Time-based routing that accounts for time zones and business hours
  • Backup assignment rules to ensure no lead ever goes unassigned

Flexible Territory Configuration

Unlike rigid systems that force you into predefined territory structures, Salesboom offers complete customization to match your unique sales organization. Group sales representatives into territories, regions, districts, or any hierarchical structure that mirrors your business model.

  • Create territories based on any criteria: geography, industry, account size, product specialization
  • Hierarchical territory structures supporting unlimited levels
  • Dynamic territories that automatically adjust as market conditions change
  • Shared territories with split credit and collaborative selling support
  • Territory inheritance rules that automatically assign child accounts
  • Multi-dimensional territories where representatives belong to multiple groups
  • Easy territory reassignment with complete history transfer
  • Territory templates for rapid deployment of new sales regions
