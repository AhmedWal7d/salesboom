The Complex Reality of Modern Transportation and Logistics

Transportation and logistics services represent global activities with complex partnering and logistical relationships, all operating within tight time constraints.

Today's competitive landscape demands that companies deliver competitive pricing while maintaining exceptional service levels to help customers enhance their own sales activities.

The Industry Faces Significant Operational Challenges:

  • Managing global operations across different regulatory systems in multiple countries
  • Coordinating complex partner networks and supply chain relationships
  • Processing high volumes of small shipments alongside lower volumes of large orders
  • Maintaining competitive pricing while ensuring profitability
  • Tracking shipments across multiple carriers and modes of transportation
  • Managing agent and claims reconciliation efficiently
  • Dealing with tight delivery windows and customer expectations for real-time updates
  • Navigating the complexity of dangerous goods and perishable freight requirements

Managing and leveraging market information and partner relationships is paramount to maintaining attractive pricing and delivery options. Without the right systems in place, transportation companies struggle with operational deficiencies, disconnected data, and missed opportunities for growth.
