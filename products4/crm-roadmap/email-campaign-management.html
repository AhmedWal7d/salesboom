Industry-Leading Deliverability Ensures Your Emails Reach Inboxes

The best email campaign is worthless if it never reaches your prospects. Salesboom maintains exceptional deliverability rates through infrastructure and best practices designed to protect your sender reputation.

Authentication Protocols

Automatic SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration ensures your emails pass authentication checks required by major email providers. We handle the technical complexity so your messages are trusted by Gmail, Outlook, and others.

Dedicated IP Options

High-volume senders can request dedicated IP addresses for complete control over sender reputation. Warm-up protocols and monitoring ensure optimal deliverability from day one.

Automatic List Hygiene

The system automatically removes bounced addresses, processes unsubscribe requests instantly, and flags potential spam traps. Maintain a clean list without manual intervention.

Reputation Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of bounce rates, spam complaints, and engagement metrics alert you to potential deliverability issues before they impact campaign performance.

Content Analysis

Pre-send spam score checking identifies problematic content, subject lines, or formatting that might trigger spam filters. Get recommendations to improve deliverability before sending.

Compliance Enforcement

Built-in CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and CASL compliance ensures every campaign includes required elements—unsubscribe links, physical addresses, consent tracking—automatically.
