Why Modern B2B Commerce Demands EDI Integration

Traditional business-to-business transactions rely on manual processes that create bottlenecks, errors, and delays. Paper-based purchase orders, invoices, and shipping notices require manual data entry, multiple handoffs, and constant follow-up calls to verify information.

Wasted Time

Hours wasted on manual data entry and verification that could be spent on strategic activities and customer relationships.

Costly Errors

Duplicate entries and transcription mistakes create costly errors that damage customer relationships and bottom-line results.

Delayed Fulfillment

Communication gaps delay order fulfillment, frustrating customers and impacting revenue recognition timelines.

Limited Visibility

Inability to track supply chain and partner transactions in real-time prevents proactive decision-making.

Partner Requirements

Large enterprises require EDI functionality, creating barriers to valuable trading partner relationships.

Scalability Issues

Manual processes don't scale as transaction volumes grow, limiting business expansion opportunities.

The Market Reality

Many large enterprises now require EDI functionality from their partners. This requirement, driven by pricing pressures and capacity needs, is accelerating the shift toward Internet-based EDI exchanges that operate in real-time rather than traditional batch processing.

Full Spectrum of EDI Transaction Types

Salesboom handles the complete range of EDI transactions required for modern B2B commerce, automating the flow of critical business documents between your organization and trading partners.

Order Management Transactions

Complete order lifecycle management from initial purchase order through final delivery confirmation:

  • Receive customer Purchase Orders (850) and Change Orders electronically
  • Generate automated Purchase Order Acknowledgments (855)
  • Send Purchase Order Change Acknowledgments
  • Provide real-time Order Status Reports
  • Deliver Customer Price Authorization Statuses

Shipping and Fulfillment Transactions

Streamline logistics and shipping communications with automated documentation:

  • Send daily Ship Notices (856) for all shipments
  • Generate Dispatch Advisements for deliveries
  • Provide real-time tracking and delivery confirmation
  • Automate carrier integration and label generation
  • Support multi-location and drop-ship scenarios

Financial Transactions

Accelerate cash flow and reduce payment processing time:

  • Send electronic Invoices (810) immediately upon shipment
  • Generate Remittance Advisements for payment processing
  • Process EDI Debit Advisements for automated clearing
  • Handle EDI Debit Advice Responses for reconciliation
  • Support multiple payment methods and banking integrations
  • Enable bank reconciliation and supply chain financing
