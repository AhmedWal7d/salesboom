Bi-Directional Data Synchronization That Just Works

The Salesboom QuickBooks Connector synchronizes critical business data between key objects in both systems, creating a unified source of truth for your organization.

Accounts to QuickBooks Customers

Customer information flows seamlessly between systems. When you create or update an Account in Salesboom CRM, the corresponding Customer record in QuickBooks updates automatically. Changes made in QuickBooks sync back to Salesboom, ensuring both systems always reflect the same accurate customer data including contact details, addresses, payment terms, and account status.

Won Opportunities to Sales Orders/Invoices

The moment you mark an Opportunity as Won in Salesboom CRM, the system automatically creates the corresponding Sales Order, Invoice, or Sales Receipt in QuickBooks. This eliminates the delay between closing a deal and beginning the fulfillment and billing process. Sales teams maintain momentum while accounting receives accurate order details instantly.

Products Synchronized with Inventory Items

Product catalogs stay perfectly aligned between both systems. When you add, update, or modify Products in Salesboom or Inventory Items in QuickBooks, changes synchronize automatically. Pricing updates, descriptions, SKU numbers, and availability status remain consistent. Sales teams always quote with current pricing while accounting maintains accurate inventory records.

Invoice Details Back to Opportunities

Financial information flows back from QuickBooks to Salesboom, completing the loop. Invoice details, payment status, outstanding balances, and transaction history appear within Opportunity records. Sales teams gain visibility into the financial relationship with each customer, enabling better conversations about renewals, upsells, and payment terms.

Extensible Integration Architecture

Beyond standard objects, the integration can be customized to sync additional fields, custom objects, or other internal systems. Whether you need to integrate custom data fields, connect to third-party applications, or build specialized workflows, our architecture supports extensions tailored to your specific business requirements.
