Smart Rules That Surface the Right Content Automatically

Simply uploading documents isn't enough. Salesboom's intelligent rules engine ensures the right marketing collateral appears automatically in the right context, eliminating guesswork and accelerating the sales process.

Default Display Rules

Set documents to automatically appear for all campaigns, contacts, accounts, leads, or opportunities as defaults. Make your company overview presentation visible on every account record, or attach your standard proposal template to all new opportunities automatically.

Conditional Display Rules

Create conditions that trigger specific collateral based on customer attributes. Show healthcare-specific case studies only to healthcare prospects. Display premium product materials only to enterprise-level opportunities.

Stage-Based Rules

Automatically present stage-appropriate materials throughout the sales cycle. Show discovery documents during initial meetings, technical specifications during evaluation, and ROI calculators during final negotiations.

Getting Started: From Upload to Sales Enablement

Implementing marketing collateral management doesn't require months of planning or complex technical projects. Follow this proven approach for fastest results.

1

Content Audit

Inventory existing materials across all locations. Categorize by type, product line, industry, and sales stage. Identify duplicates and select current versions.

2

Upload & Structure

Upload materials with clear folder structures and naming conventions. Add metadata tags for enhanced searchability. Test search functionality.

3

Rules Configuration

Define display rules that automatically surface relevant materials. Set defaults and configure conditional rules based on customer context.

4

Team Training

Conduct training sessions on accessing, searching, and sharing collateral. Demonstrate mobile access and gather initial feedback.

5

Optimization

Monitor usage analytics and identify high-performing materials. Continuously refine rules based on real-world usage patterns.
