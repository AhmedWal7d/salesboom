Powerful Features That Simplify Inventory Management

Salesboom's inventory management system provides the robust functionality businesses need to maintain optimal stock levels while reducing administrative overhead.

Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Every product in your catalog maintains accurate, up-to-the-minute stock levels visible across your entire organization. Sales representatives can check availability instantly before making commitments to customers. Warehouse staff see real-time updates as products move in and out. Financial teams access current inventory valuations for accurate reporting.

  • Current stock levels by location and warehouse
  • Reserved inventory allocated to pending orders
  • Available inventory for new sales opportunities
  • Inventory in transit between locations
  • Historical inventory movements and adjustments
  • Product serial numbers and lot tracking
  • Inventory valuation and cost of goods sold

Automatic Inventory Level Updates

Configure scheduled inventory replenishment for products that arrive on predictable schedules. If your warehouse receives stock deliveries weekly, monthly, or on any custom interval, Salesboom automatically adjusts inventory levels according to your schedule.

Set the replenishment frequency once, and the system handles the updates automaticallyeliminating manual data entry and ensuring inventory records stay accurate without constant oversight. You can also configure different schedules for different product categories or suppliers, accommodating the unique requirements of your supply chain.

Manual Inventory Adjustments

Real-world inventory management requires flexibility for unexpected situations. Salesboom provides straightforward manual adjustment capabilities for those times when reality doesn't match the schedule.

Handle unexpected scenarios easily:

  • Larger than anticipated customer purchase depletes stock
  • Damaged inventory discovered during quality inspection
  • Stock transfers between warehouse locations
  • Physical inventory count reveals discrepancies
  • Returns or exchanges that affect available stock
  • Emergency shipments from suppliers

Manual adjustments are logged with full audit trails showing who made the change, when, and whymaintaining accountability while providing operational flexibility.

Never Miss a Sale Due to Stockouts Again

Salesboom's intelligent alert system monitors inventory levels continuously and proactively notifies the right people before products run out. This advanced warning system is completely customizable to match your business processes and organizational structure.

Employee Notifications

Configure alerts to notify specific employees or entire teams when inventory falls below defined thresholds. Send notifications to purchasing managers, warehouse supervisors, sales directors, or anyone who needs to know about low stock conditions.

Alerts can trigger at multiple levelsperhaps a warning at 20% remaining stock and an urgent alert at 5%giving teams adequate time to reorder before inventory runs out completely.

Customer Notifications

Automatically notify customers when products they've purchased previously or expressed interest in are running low. This proactive communication encourages reorders before stockouts occur and demonstrates attentive service that strengthens customer relationships.

Customers appreciate knowing when they should place orders to ensure product availability, and you benefit from more predictable demand and reduced last-minute rush orders.

Flexible Alert Configuration

Set different alert thresholds for different products based on importance, lead time, or demand patterns. Fast-moving items might trigger alerts at higher stock levels than slow-moving products. Critical components with long lead times can have earlier warnings than readily available items.

Choose delivery methods for alerts: email, SMS, in-system notifications, or all three. Escalate urgent alerts to ensure they receive attention. Configure quiet hours to prevent notifications during off-hours unless truly urgent.

Manage Multiple Warehouse Locations Effortlessly

Businesses with multiple warehouse locations, distribution centers, or retail storefronts require sophisticated inventory tracking that maintains accuracy across all facilities. Salesboom's multi-warehouse inventory management provides complete visibility and control regardless of how many locations you operate.

Location-Specific Stock Levels

Track inventory quantities separately for each warehouse, store, or distribution center. View aggregate totals across all locations or drill down to specific facility inventory. Transfer stock between locations and track inventory in transit.

Intelligent Order Routing

When customers place orders, the system can automatically select the optimal fulfillment location based on proximity, stock availability, and shipping costs. Reduce delivery times and shipping expenses by fulfilling orders from the nearest warehouse with available inventory.

Inter-Location Transfers

Move inventory between warehouses to rebalance stock levels or fulfill urgent customer needs. Track transfer requests, approvals, shipment status, and receipt confirmation. Maintain accurate inventory records throughout the transfer process.

Location-Based Analytics

Compare performance metrics across warehouses to identify optimization opportunities. Analyze turnover rates, carrying costs, and fulfillment efficiency by location. Make data-driven decisions about inventory distribution and warehouse operations.

Location-Specific Permissions

Control which employees can view or modify inventory at specific locations. Warehouse managers might have full control over their facility while only viewing inventory at other locations. Corporate leadership sees everything.

Custom Workflows

Configure different operational processes for different locations. Some warehouses might use bin locations and barcode scanning while others use simpler systems. Each location can operate according to its specific requirements.

Seamless Integration with Sales Operations

The power of Salesboom's inventory management comes from its deep integration with CRM and sales processes. When inventory, opportunities, quotes, and orders work together seamlessly, sales teams operate more efficiently and customers receive better service.

Real-Time Availability During Sales

Sales representatives view current inventory levels directly within opportunity and quote interfaces. Before promising delivery dates or quantities, they confirm actual product availability. This real-time visibility prevents the embarrassing situation of selling products you don't have.

When creating quotes, the system shows which products are in stock, which require ordering, and estimated availability dates for out-of-stock items. Sales teams can make realistic commitments that build customer trust rather than overpromising and disappointing.

Automatic Inventory Reservation

When opportunities reach certain stages or quotes are accepted, the system can automatically reserve inventory for those orders. This prevents double-booking where multiple sales representatives promise the same inventory to different customers.

Reserved inventory reduces available stock for other orders while remaining allocated to the specific opportunity until the sale closes or the reservation expires. This sophisticated inventory allocation ensures accurate availability information across the entire sales team.

Order Fulfillment Workflow

When sales close, orders automatically reduce inventory levels and notify warehouse staff about items requiring shipment. The system generates pick lists, packing slips, and shipping labelsstreamlining the path from closed sale to shipped product.

Track order fulfillment status in real-time. Sales representatives and customers see when orders are picked, packed, and shipped without requiring manual updates. This visibility improves customer communication and reduces "where's my order?" inquiries.

Powerful Analytics That Drive Inventory Optimization

Transform inventory data into actionable insights with comprehensive analytics and reporting capabilities. Make informed decisions about purchasing, stocking levels, and product strategies based on actual data rather than intuition.

Inventory Turnover Analysis

Calculate turnover rates for every product, category, or location. Identify slow-moving inventory that ties up capital and fast-moving products requiring increased stock levels. Optimize purchasing decisions based on actual movement patterns.

Stock Level Optimization

Analyze historical demand patterns to determine optimal stock levels that balance carrying costs with service level objectives. Find the sweet spot between having too much inventory (expensive) and too little (lost sales).

Demand Forecasting

Predict future inventory requirements based on sales trends, seasonality, and growth projections. Plan purchases proactively rather than reactively, ensuring adequate stock without excessive carrying costs.

Inventory Valuation

Calculate total inventory value using various costing methods (FIFO, LIFO, weighted average). Track inventory investment by location, category, or supplier. Generate reports for financial planning and tax purposes.

Stockout Analysis

Track instances where inventory ran out and sales were potentially lost. Quantify the revenue impact of stockouts to justify increased inventory investment where appropriate. Identify patterns indicating need for higher safety stock.

Profit Margin Analysis

Compare inventory costs with sales prices to identify high-margin and low-margin products. Make strategic decisions about product focus, pricing adjustments, and inventory investment allocation based on profitability data.
