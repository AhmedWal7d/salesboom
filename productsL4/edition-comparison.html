How to Migrate from Salesforce to Salesboom

Switching CRM systems sounds daunting, but Salesboom makes the transition smooth and stress-free.

1

Trial and Evaluation

Start with Salesboom's free 30-day trial. Explore the interface, test key features, and verify the system meets your needs. Our team is available throughout the trial to answer questions and demonstrate capabilities.

2

Data Export from Salesforce

Export your contacts, accounts, opportunities, and historical data from Salesforce. Standard data export tools create CSV files containing all your information. Our team provides specific instructions to ensure nothing important is missed.

3

Data Mapping and Import

Work with our data specialists to map Salesforce fields to Salesboom fields. We handle custom field creation, data transformation, and import execution. Most organizations complete full data migration in 2-3 business days.

4

Team Training

Schedule training sessions for your team covering key workflows, best practices, and features you'll use daily. Recorded sessions allow new team members to get up to speed quickly.

5

Go Live

Switch your team to Salesboom on a predetermined date. For the first week, we monitor usage, answer questions, and ensure everything operates smoothly. Most teams reach full productivity within 5-7 business days.

6

Cancel Salesforce

Once you're successfully using Salesboom, cancel your Salesforce subscription and redirect those savings toward growing your business instead of overpaying for CRM software.

Risk-Free Migration

Run both systems in parallel during transition if needed. Keep your Salesforce data accessible until you're completely confident in the migration. Our team helps troubleshoot any issues that arise during the switchover period. We make switching CRM providers as painless as possible.
