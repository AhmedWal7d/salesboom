Advanced Capabilities Found Only in Enterprise Edition

Unlock enterprise-exclusive features that go beyond standard CRM functionality.

Service Quotes Management

Generate professional service quotes on the fly and deliver them instantly. The system creates polished PDF quotes and emails them directly from within Salesboom. Tightly integrated with contract management and time billing, quotes connect seamlessly with opportunity managementwhen an opportunity closes, billing software instantly creates and emails invoices.

Associate contracts with quotes, contacts, accounts, and opportunities for complete visibility. Your sales team concentrates on selling, not wrestling with quote documents.

Inventory Database Program

Gain a 360-degree view of complete inventorypast, present, and future. Know which products resonate with which customer types. Provide your sales team with quick product reference so they know exactly what's offered and at what cost.

Create online product catalogs with special discount pricing. Compare customer reports against product reports to determine key selling opportunities and optimize inventory levels. Never overstock or understock critical items again.

Contract Management System

Manage contracts in a web-based environment accessible 24/7/365 with no software to install. Customize data storage about your contracts, import from existing systems, and receive real-time alerts when contracts require attention.

Enable self-service contract creation from your website. Apply mass updates when needed. Associate contracts with events, tasks, calls, notes, documents, emails, accounts, opportunities, campaigns, and more.

Commissions Management

Automate commission calculations based on sales performance, product categories, and custom business rules. Track commission payments, generate reports, and provide sales reps with real-time visibility into their earnings.

Eliminate disputes and manual calculations while motivating your team with transparent, accurate commission tracking that updates automatically as deals close.
