Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure for Reliable Delivery

Email deliverability requires robust infrastructure and sender reputation management. Salesboom maintains enterprise-grade email sending infrastructure with high deliverability rates that ensure your messages reach customer inboxes, not spam folders.

Deliverability Features

  • Dedicated IP addresses available for high-volume senders
  • Authentication protocols including SPF, DKIM, and DMARC
  • Bounce management that automatically cleans invalid addresses
  • Spam testing tools to evaluate content before sending
  • Feedback loop monitoring to track spam complaints

  • Compliance with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and international regulations

  • Disaster recovery and business continuity planning

  • Protection against emerging threats
  • Complete data privacy and compliance assurance
