Enterprise Edition: Comprehensive CRM for Growing Organizations

The Enterprise Edition represents the most comprehensive Salesboom CRM solution, purpose-built for larger small businesses and mid-market companies operating in networked environments with multiple divisions, teams, and complex workflows.

Who Should Choose Enterprise Edition

Enterprise Edition is the optimal choice for businesses that:

  • Operate with multiple departments or business units requiring separate data views
  • Need advanced customer self-service capabilities to reduce support workload
  • Run multi-channel marketing campaigns requiring automation and tracking
  • Require integration with existing business applications through XML connectivity
  • Manage teams across different locations or regions
  • Need extensive customization with up to 250 customizable tabs
  • Want optional offline functionality for field teams
  • Require wireless mobile access for on-the-go sales and service teams

Sales Force Automation

Complete pipeline management, opportunity tracking, quote generation, forecasting, and territory management across multiple sales teams and regions.

Customer Service & Support

Case management with escalation procedures, knowledge base, SLAs, customer portal for self-service, and comprehensive ticket tracking.

Marketing Automation

Campaign management tools for planning, executing, and measuring multi-channel marketing initiatives including email campaigns and lead nurturing.

Self-Service Portal

Branded customer portal allowing clients to submit tickets, track case status, access knowledge base, and manage their account independently.

XML Integration Framework

Open architecture enabling seamless connectivity with ERP systems, accounting software, marketing platforms, and custom applications.

Advanced Customization

Up to 250 customizable tabs, fields, and layouts allowing businesses to tailor the CRM to match exact processes without custom development.

Professional Edition: Full-Featured CRM Without the Complexity

The Professional Edition delivers enterprise-class CRM functionality tailored for non-departmental small businesses that need sophisticated tools without the overhead of features designed for large, complex organizations.

Who Should Choose Professional Edition

Professional Edition is ideal for businesses that:

  • Operate as a single cohesive team rather than multiple departments
  • Need complete CRM functionality for sales, service, and customer management
  • Want robust customization options with up to 100 customizable tabs
  • Don't require customer-facing self-service portals
  • Manage marketing through direct outreach rather than complex campaigns
  • Need standard integrations but not XML-based custom connectivity
  • Prefer streamlined simplicity over maximum feature density
  • Value cost efficiency while maintaining professional-grade capabilities

What's Included in Professional Edition

Complete Sales Management: Lead tracking, opportunity management, pipeline visualization, quote creation, contact management, and sales forecasting tools.

Customer Service Tools: Case management, ticket tracking, customer communication history, service level monitoring, and internal collaboration features.

Contact & Account Management: 360-degree customer view, interaction history, document management, relationship tracking, and detailed customer profiles.

Reporting & Analytics: Customizable reports, dashboards, data export capabilities, and performance tracking across all CRM activities.

Team Edition: Essential CRM for Small Teams

The Team Edition provides focused CRM functionality specifically designed for small teams of 6-10 users who need core customer relationship management capabilities without the complexity of enterprise features.

Perfect for Small Teams

Team Edition is designed for:

  • Startups and small businesses with 6-10 team members
  • Teams focused on core sales and customer management
  • Businesses seeking affordable entry point to CRM
  • Organizations that prioritize simplicity and ease of use
  • Teams ready to move beyond spreadsheets and email
  • Businesses planning to grow into more comprehensive editions

Core Capabilities

Despite its streamlined focus, Team Edition delivers essential CRM functionality including contact management, account tracking, sales pipeline management, activity logging, basic reporting, email integration, and mobile access—everything small teams need to organize customer relationships effectively.
