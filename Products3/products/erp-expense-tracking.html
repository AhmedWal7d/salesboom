Streamline Approvals with Intelligent Workflow Automation

Salesboom eliminates the bottlenecks and delays of manual expense approval through configurable workflow automation. Managers receive instant notifications, can review complete expense details with attached receipts, and approve or reject reports with a single click from anywhere.

Budget-Based Automatic Rejection

Track employee spending against budgeted amounts and automatically reject unauthorized expenses that exceed allocated budgets. The system compares each expense against departmental budgets, project allocations, and spending policies in real-time, flagging or blocking expenses that violate established limits before they're approved.

Multi-Level Approval Routing

Configure approval hierarchies based on expense amount, type, or department. Small expenses can be auto-approved or require single-level approval, while larger expenditures automatically route through multiple approval stages including department managers, finance directors, and executives.

Real-Time Notification System

Managers receive instant email or SMS notifications when expense reports require attention. Notifications include expense summaries, budget impact, and direct links to approve or reject with comments. No more delayed reimbursements due to reports sitting in email inboxes.

Rejection with Comments

Reject unauthorized or improperly documented expenses online immediately with explanatory comments. Employees receive instant feedback about why expenses were rejected and what documentation or corrections are needed, eliminating confusion and back-and-forth communication.

How Different Teams Leverage Expense Tracking

Salesboom's expense tracking solution serves diverse business needs across departments and functions.

Sales Teams

Sales representatives track travel expenses, client entertainment, and opportunity-specific costs directly associated with deals in progress. Sales managers approve expenses while reviewing opportunity status, understand customer acquisition costs, and make informed decisions about resource allocation to high-value prospects.

Project Teams
