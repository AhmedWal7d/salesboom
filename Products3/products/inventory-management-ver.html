Integrated Inventory Management That Works Seamlessly With Your CRM

Salesboom delivers a fully integrated extensive inventory management software solution that allows you to track inventory levels and assets online through both manual and automated inventory management systems.

Unlike standalone inventory tools that create data silos, our inventory management is seamlessly integrated into the CRM and SFA solution, creating a unified platform for your entire business.

Real-Time Data Stream

This ERP software suite features real-time data stream inventory management that automatically updates as sales are made. Set automated inventory level updates on a recurring basis for always-accurate stock levels.

Web-Based Access

From any PC with internet access, view and manage your web-based inventory management software, keeping your small, medium, or large business ahead with complete visibility.

Single Platform Data

Since your information is held on a single platform, you can quickly reference all inventory levels over any period of time—data is documented from day one of implementation.

Real-Time Visibility Across Your Entire Inventory

Manual inventory tracking through spreadsheets and disconnected systems leads to stock-outs, overselling, and costly errors. Salesboom eliminates these problems with real-time inventory tracking.

Automatic Updates As Sales Occur

The moment a sale is completed, inventory levels update automatically across the system. Sales teams see accurate stock availability instantly, preventing overselling and customer disappointment.

This automatic synchronization eliminates the lag time between sales and inventory updates that plague businesses using separate systems. No more end-of-day batch updates or manual data entry.

Scheduled Automated Updates

Beyond transaction-based updates, Salesboom allows you to set recurring automated inventory level updates. Schedule daily, weekly, or monthly inventory counts and reconciliations.

Set automatic reorder triggers when stock falls below defined thresholds. Program seasonal adjustments that account for predictable demand patterns. These automated updates reduce manual work while ensuring inventory accuracy.

Data-Driven Inventory Decisions Through Comprehensive Analytics

Transform inventory data into actionable insights with Salesboom's powerful reporting and analytics capabilities.

Inventory Valuation Reports

Track total inventory value by location, category, or vendor. Monitor how inventory investment changes over time. Generate reports for accounting and financial planning with FIFO, LIFO, and weighted average cost methods.

Stock Level Reports

View current stock levels across all locations with drill-down capability. Identify items below reorder points requiring immediate attention. Forecast when popular items will run out based on sales velocity.

Inventory Turnover Analysis

Calculate inventory turnover rates by product, category, or location. Identify slow-moving inventory that ties up capital. Discover fast-moving items that should receive increased investment.

Purchase Order Reports

Track open purchase orders and expected delivery dates. Analyze vendor performance including on-time delivery rates. Identify cost-saving opportunities through vendor comparison.

Inventory Movement Reports

Monitor inventory transfers between locations. Track receiving, picking, and shipping activities. Analyze seasonal trends and demand patterns to optimize stocking strategies.

Shrinkage and Accuracy Reports

Identify inventory discrepancies between system counts and physical counts. Calculate shrinkage rates by location and category. Track count accuracy by employee to improve accountability.

All reports are customizable, exportable to Excel or PDF, and can be scheduled for automatic delivery to stakeholders.
