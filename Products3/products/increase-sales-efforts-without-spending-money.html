Complete Sales Force Automation Tools Included

Salesboom's Cloud CRM delivers a comprehensive suite of sales management tools that cover every aspect of the sales process from initial lead to closed deal and beyond.

Lead Management

Capture leads from multiple sources including web forms, email campaigns, trade shows, and partner referrals. Score and qualify leads automatically based on engagement and demographic criteria. Route hot leads to the right sales rep instantly.

Contact Management

Maintain complete profiles for every contact including communication history, preferences, relationships, and buying signals. Link contacts to accounts and opportunities for full relationship visibility.

Account Management

Track all accounts with hierarchical views of complex organizational structures. Monitor account health scores and identify at-risk relationships before they churn. Document account strategies and competitive threats.

Opportunity Management

Manage sales opportunities through customizable pipeline stages from qualification to close. Track deal size, probability, expected close date, and competitive positioning. Identify bottlenecks and stalled deals.

Quote Management

Generate professional quotes quickly with product catalogs, pricing rules, and discount approvals. Track quote versions, status, and acceptance rates. Convert approved quotes to orders seamlessly.

Sales Forecasting

Forecast revenue with confidence using historical win rates and trend analysis. Build multiple forecast scenarios for best case, worst case, and most likely outcomes. Track forecast accuracy over time.

Activity Management

Schedule and track all sales activities including calls, emails, meetings, and tasks. Set reminders to ensure consistent follow-through. View activity history to understand engagement patterns.

Sales Analytics

Analyze sales performance with customizable dashboards and reports. Track KPIs including pipeline velocity, win rates, average deal size, and sales cycle length. Identify top performers and coaching opportunities.

Mobile CRM

Access complete CRM functionality from iOS and Android devices. Update records, log activities, and check pipeline status from anywhere. Work offline with automatic sync when reconnected.
